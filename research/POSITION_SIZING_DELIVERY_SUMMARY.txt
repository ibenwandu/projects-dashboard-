KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. YOU CANNOT FIX 0% WIN RATE WITH POSITION SIZING
   ➜ System must fix trading logic first
   ➜ Position sizing only amplifies positive edge
   ➜ Critical: Implement stop losses before anything else

2. PROFESSIONAL STANDARD: RISK 1-2% PER TRADE
   ➜ Conservative: 0.5-1% (Phase 1 for your situation)
   ➜ Standard: 1-2% (Phase 2 after 40% win rate proven)
   ➜ Dangerous: >3% (account blowup risk)

3. POSITION SIZE FORMULA (MEMORIZE THIS)
   Position Units = (Account × Risk%) / (Stop Loss Pips × Pip Value)
   Example: ($100 × 0.005) / (10 × 0.0001) = 500 units

4. WIN RATE < RISK-REWARD RATIO
   ➜ Can be profitable with 25% win rate if R:R is 1:3
   ➜ Can be profitable with 40% win rate if R:R is 1:2
   ➜ Cannot be profitable with 0% win rate (your current state)

5. DAILY LOSS LIMITS PREVENT "REVENGE TRADING"
   ➜ Stop after losing 2% of account in a day
   ➜ This single rule prevents most account blowups
   ➜ Psychological protection as much as financial

6. CIRCUIT BREAKER: 5 CONSECUTIVE LOSSES = STOP
   ➜ Indicates system needs adjustment
   ➜ Prevents cascading losses
   ➜ Forces structured break and review

7. ACCOUNT GROWTH IS AUTOMATIC
   ➜ Position size scales with account growth
   ➜ No manual adjustment needed
   ➜ At 0.5% risk: $100 → $127/year (conservative)
   ➜ At 1% risk: $100 → $180/year (moderate)
   ➜ At 2% risk: $100 → realistic ~$420/year (with Phase scaling)

8. MOST ACCOUNT BLOWUPS CAUSED BY:
   ➜ No stop losses (40% of failures)
   ➜ Position size increases after losses (20%)
   ➜ Ignoring daily loss limits (15%)
   ➜ Over-leveraging (15%)
   ➜ Wrong risk-reward ratio (10%)

## 📋 HOW TO APPLY

### Online Applications
Most benefits require you to:
1. Have a Social Insurance Number (SIN)
2. File your taxes annually (even with no income)
3. Register for CRA My Account

### Key Links
- [CRA My Account](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/e-services-individuals/account-individuals.html) - Manage your benefits online
- [Service Canada My Account](https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/services/my-account.html) - Apply for EI, CPP, OAS

## 💡 IMPORTANT TIPS

### Filing Taxes is Key
- **You must file taxes to receive most benefits** - even if you have no income
- File by April 30 each year (June 15 if self-employed)
- Benefits are calculated based on your previous year's tax return

### Payment Schedule
- **GST/HST Credit:** Quarterly (January, April, July, October)
- **Canada Child Benefit:** Monthly
- **Canada Carbon Rebate:** Quarterly (January, April, July, October)
- **CPP/OAS:** Monthly

### Income Thresholds
Most benefits are income-tested. Check each program for specific limits.

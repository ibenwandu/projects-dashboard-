## Full job description

Charger Logistics Inc. is a world-class asset-based carrier with locations across North America. With over 20 years of experience providing the best logistics solutions, Charger Logistics has transformed into a world-class transport provider and continues to grow.

We are currently looking to add a motivated individual that is able to work in a fast-paced environment to join our Warehouse team.

Job Responsibilities{{{{:}}}}


Respond to inquiries from customers and Customer Service regarding rush orders, inventory discrepancies, scheduling rework / builds
Review customer orders received. Check for accuracy and completeness
Interpret, manage and coordinate with the warehouse on any specific order requirements
Plan then release orders to the Warehouse Team according to customer schedules and / or needs
Arrange pickups for customers. Book appointments online via customer portals and email. Track all pickups to ensure they have occurred
Update customers' website portals for shipping and cancel date details
Plan and book inbound delivery appointments. Assign drivers to doors for pick up and deliveries
Manage customer on-site pickups, provide BOL's and coordinate loading of orders with the Warehouse team
Keep all parties informed, communicating issues, delays and options. Problem solve as required
Sort, distribute and file shipping and receiving documents
Confirm inventory with Inventory team, relay any issues with Customer Service, ensure timelines are being met and coordinate shipments to and from the warehouse and off-site facility. Resolve any issues and escalate if required


Requirements


Related warehouse clerk or warehouse administrative experience is required
Experience working with major retailers in a supply chain / logistics environment is an asset
Invoicing and manpower resource planning experience are assets
Ability to effectively communicate both verbally and in writing
Ability to prioritize and manage conflicting demands
Ability to work individually as well as part of a team
Demonstrated time management skills

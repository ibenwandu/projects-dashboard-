## Full job description

See What You Can Do With Prophix

Prophix helps finance teams work with greater flexibility and confidence through Prophix One™, our Financial Performance Platform. We bring planning, reporting, and automation together so people can focus on higher-value work instead of repetitive tasks. As we introduce more AI-enabled capabilities, you will be part of a human-centered innovation journey where intelligent tools support better outcomes while people lead the decisions that matter.

We're headquartered in the Greater Toronto Area, with teams and offices across North America, Europe, and Australia.

As Prophix continues its journey to becoming a fully data-enabled business, we are seeking a Senior Manager, Data & Analytics to help lead and scale a modern, enterprise-wide analytics capability. In this strategic and high-impact role, you will shape how data is leveraged to drive business outcomes—from BI dashboards and AI solutions, to data-driven decision-making across teams. Reporting to the Senior Director, Finance & Rev Ops, you'll play a pivotal role in building both the team and the foundation that powers Prophix's transformation into a data-first organization.

Requirements

What You Will Do


Lead and manage the execution of Prophix's data and analytics strategy to enable data-driven decision making
Build, coach, and lead a team of data professionals to deliver strategic and tactical data solutions
Partner with senior leaders across functions to gather requirements, prioritize needs, and define measurable outcomes
Design and oversee the architecture and operations of Prophix's data infrastructure, including data warehousing, MDM, data governance, and reporting platforms
Champion a culture of data literacy and experimentation across the company
Operationalize advanced analytics capabilities, including dashboards, predictive models, and self-service reporting
Define and implement enterprise-wide policies for data governance, security, lineage, and quality
Collaborate cross-functionally to integrate analytics into core business workflows and KPIs
Track and report progress on key initiatives and delivered business value to stakeholders
Ensure compliance with data regulations and advocate for ethical data usage


What You Will Bring

If this role excites you but you don't meet every requirement, we still encourage you to apply. At Prophix, curiosity, adaptability, and diverse perspectives matter. Your experience may be exactly what we need.

Required Qualifications


5+ years of senior leadership experience in data and analytics, including data governance and data platform management
Proven experience building and leading cross-functional data teams in fast-paced, complex environments
Demonstrated success translating strategic goals into actionable analytics initiatives with measurable impact
Hands-on experience with data ecosystems including Snowflake, Tableau, Power BI, Matillion, and Azure
Strong understanding of data lifecycle management, architecture, metadata, lineage, and MDM
Track record of delivering enterprise-wide dashboards, AI/ML models, and self-service analytics tools
Effective stakeholder engagement skills across technical and non-technical audiences, including C-level executives
Deep experience with Agile methodologies, modern SDLC practices, and cloud-based data solutions
Strong communication and presentation skills with the ability to simplify complex topics
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Data Analytics, Engineering, or Business; MBA or Master's preferred
Comfort using AI tools responsibly to support tasks such as research, drafting, or data review
Curiosity and openness to exploring new approaches
Collaborative mindset when working with teams and technology
Must be legally entitled to work in the country where this role is located


Preferred Qualifications


Experience driving large-scale digital transformation in a global SaaS or tech company.
Ability to create momentum in ambiguous situations and lead through influence
Familiarity with data privacy regulations and ethical AI principles
Experience embedding data into customer-facing products or services
Passion for creating accessible, inclusive data practices and tools

## Full job description

Cooksville Hyundai is looking for a Sales Representative to add to their team in Mississauga.

The Sales Representative is responsible for selling and leasing new and pre-owned vehicles and creating lasting relationships with customers.

Purchasing a vehicle is an exciting time! Our Sales Representatives walk customers through the sales process, including conducting test drivers, reviewing vehicle prices and educating customers on vehicle inventory.

Cooksville Hyundai are proud members of The Humberview Group - one of Ontario's leading automotive dealer groups and winner of Canada's Best Managed Companies program for 2024 and 2025. As automotive specialists, we represent 5000 vehicles. 20 Stores. 18 Brands. And 1 Promise: Serving you better, each and everyday.

Please note, this position is open only to individuals who are legally entitled to work in Canada at the time of application and in the future. The Humberview Group does not provide sponsorship for work permits or immigration support.

Compensation: Commission-based only, ranging from $37,000 to $90,000 a year - potential to earn more based on performance.

Job Duties


Provide customers with excellent customer service and information regarding vehicles for sale.
Always maintain professionalism.
Conduct test drives of vehicles for sale.
Complete sales of vehicles while offering additional products and/or services.
Build and foster a network of referrals to create new opportunities for revenue growth.
Performs other duties as required.


What We Look For


Valid OMVIC certification is required.
Valid G driver's license.
Previous automotive sales experience is preferred.
Professional demeanor, selling style, and appearance.
Exceptional verbal communication and presentation skills.
Excellent listening skills.
Self-motivated, with high energy and an engaging level of enthusiasm.
Ability to work individually and as part of a team.
High level of integrity, work ethic and attention to detail.

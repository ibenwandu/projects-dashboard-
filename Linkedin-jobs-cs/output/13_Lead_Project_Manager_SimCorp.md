WHY THIS ROLE IS IMPORTANT TO US

As an Onboarding Project Manager at SimCorp, you will play a key role in enabling some of the world’s largest financial institutions to smoothly transition to our Azure-hosted SaaS platform. You will lead end‑to‑end infrastructure onboarding projects across the Americas, coordinating closely with Sales, Consulting Services, SaaS Onboard Operations, and SaaS Innovations teams to design, build, deploy, and launch client environments. By managing client engagement, resources, finances, and complex stakeholders, you will ensure smooth go‑lives, reduced risk, and effective customer outcomes—while being supported by a global community of Project Management professionals as you grow your career at SimCorp.

What You Will Be Responsible For


Managing all aspects of the SimCorp Infrastructure Onboarding process: Delivering projects using our standard agile methods across all project phases including Dawn, Design, Swarm, Develop, Deploy, Dissect and Deliver through client Go-live
Managing multiple Onboarding projects and related workstreams - identifying all required tasks, dependencies, and time/resource estimations as well as providing regular status updates
Managing handovers from Sales to Onboarding and from Onboarding to Run Operations from an infrastructure perspective
Facilitating business requirements gathering workshops
Contributing to improvement and documentation of best practices for Onboarding projects globally in collaboration with Global PMO
Creating and maintaining required project artefacts such as project plan, reports and RAID log, status reports and the change management process
Effectively monitoring customer feedback and reporting
Consistently identifying and mitigating risk
Motivating the project team and ensuring continuous improvement within the team
Maintaining and updating project standard artifacts, processes and procedures

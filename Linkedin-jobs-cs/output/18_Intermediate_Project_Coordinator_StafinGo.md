## Full job description

Hiring: Project Coordinator (IT Projects)




Location: Hybrid (Up to 3 days onsite, 2 days home)

Duration: 1 year with the option to extend an additional year

Compensation: $45.00 – $60.00 per hour

Submissions Deadline: Wednesday, March 11th @ noon




Position Summary:

Stafingo is seeking an experienced Project Coordinator to provide leadership and technical delivery support for IT projects. The successful candidate will coordinate small to medium-sized projects, support project teams, and assist Project Managers in delivering medium to large Information & Technology initiatives.




This role requires excellent communication, strong analytical skills, and the ability to work effectively both independently and within a multidisciplinary team.




Key Responsibilities:

Project Documentation & Reporting:

Maintain and update project plans, schedules, and timelines.
Prepare status reports, meeting agendas, and minutes.
Maintain risk and issue logs and document control for contracts and invoices.

Operational & Administrative Support:

Track resource allocation, team availability, and equipment usage.
Support workflow management to ensure deadlines are met.
Serve as liaison with vendors and internal/external stakeholders.

Logistics & Coordination:

Schedule and coordinate meetings and project workshops.
Support onboarding and training for new project team members.
Assist in procurement processes and contract management.

Financial & Project Controls:

Monitor project budgets and control expenditures.
Provide input on project decisions, resource allocation, and staffing levels.

Leadership & Team Support:

Provide guidance and direction to project teams.
Support recruitment of project delivery staff and coordinate project activities.
Facilitate the collection of business requirements and build consensus among stakeholders.




Must-Have Qualifications:

Experience coordinating IT projects across all stages of the project lifecycle.
Proven ability to draft project management documents (SOW, project charters, WBS, risk management plans, financial documents, RFI/RFQ/RFP).
Experience with collaborative project management tools (e.g., Microsoft Enterprise Project Management, Microsoft Project).
Strong presentation and negotiation skills with stakeholders at all levels.
Experience supervising and motivating staff in a matrix environment.
Business and IT analysis experience, including feasibility studies and recommendations.
Proficiency in project scheduling, resource estimation, and critical path identification.
Knowledge of procurement processes and vendor management.
Demonstrated ability to manage project budgets and control costs.
Excellent interpersonal, facilitation, and problem-solving skills.
PMP or CAPM certification is an asset.

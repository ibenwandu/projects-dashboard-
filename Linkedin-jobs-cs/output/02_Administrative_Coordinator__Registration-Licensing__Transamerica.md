Job Description Summary

The Registration Administrator will provide administrative support to a national sales force distributing financial service products, with a focus on the mutual fund registrations of applicants. Administer and assist field agents to obtain mutual fund licenses and maintain the accuracy of WFG Securities Inc. registration records.

Responsibilities

Job Description


Facilitate and support initial, reactivation, and reinstatement applications for mutual funds licenses.
Follow-up and document National Registration Database (NRD) submissions, including outside business activity (OBAs), address updates, financial disclosures, etc.
Enter and record mutual fund registration terminations in the system and notify necessary parties.
Perform NRD annual renewals.
Participate in the Annual Disclosure process.
Update of NRD training documentation.
Update of various internal systems.
Respond to inquiries from field, internal and external staff and regulatory bodies.
Provide administrative support to field agents, and internal and external staff.
Follow file management and record management procedures.
Draft standard and non-standard correspondence as required.
Initiate and participate in process and database improvements.
Act in a registration support capacity for the Compliance department.
Support projects and audits as requested.


Qualifications

Interpersonal and customer service skills to build relationships with internal and external contacts
Excellent organizational and multi-tasking skills

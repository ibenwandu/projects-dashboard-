Sales Representative- Southwestern Ontario

CGC Inc. is a leading marketer, manufacturer and distributor of gypsum wallboard products, interior finishing material and suspended acoustical ceilings. With locations around the world, CGC and USG have the resources, plants, products, and people to build walls, ceilings, and careers.

CGC Inc. is currently recruiting Sales Representative in the Southwestern Ontario region. The ideal location for the successful candidate to live is London, Ontario.

This is a challenging opportunity for a motivated individual to demonstrate their selling and customer service skills in a fast-paced sales environment.

Duties And Responsibilities Of The Sales Representative Include

Reporting to the Sales Manager- Ontario, the successful candidate will be responsible for promotion, sales, and service of our products to dealers, distributors, and contractors.

With a passion for customer service and commitment to follow through, the Sales Representative is responsible for growing sales volumes by building relationships and providing the required support to dealers, distributors, and contractors. Key in this effort will be influencing customer loyalty by delivering a high level of customer service via proactive customer contact, industry knowledge and networking, product training, deep technical product knowledge, large project coordination, job quotes and prompt complaint resolution.

Developing strong relationships with internal departments including marketing, distribution, customer service and accounting is also critical for success in this role. The Sales Representative works within general guidelines and procedures, exercising independent judgment while being committed to safety, quality, reliability, and customer service.

The ideal candidate will have experience in sales, customer service and/or marketing (preferably in the building materials industry) and a post-secondary education in Business, Marketing, or a related field.

This challenging position offers a competitive compensation package and a company auto. The base salary is dependent upon education and experience.

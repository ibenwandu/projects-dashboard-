## Full job description

(Event-Based Sales & Promotions)

Location: Toronto, ON

Do you love representing brands and creating memorable customer experiences?

Are you confident, outgoing, and looking for a role where your personality makes an impact?




About Us

At Westpoint Acquisitions, we connect leading Canadian brands with their customers through dynamic, face-to-face interactions. From pop-up events to retail credit card activations, our team delivers energy, authenticity, and results.




The Opportunity

As a Brand Ambassador, you’ll represent top-tier national brands at live events, retail promotions, and community activations. You’ll build brand awareness, engage customers, and help deliver an unforgettable experience.




What You’ll Do

Represent leading Canadian brands at pop-up events, retail promotions, and community activations, helping to create memorable, in-person experiences that leave a lasting impression.
Approach and engage customers in a friendly, approachable way — starting conversations, building trust, and helping customers understand product benefits and special offers.
Deliver credit card product demonstrations and presentations to highlight key features and services, while tailoring your approach to meet individual customer needs.
Provide accurate product information and confidently answer customer questions, helping them make informed decisions in a positive, pressure-free environment.
Assist with event setup and takedown, ensuring displays, signage, and promotional materials are professional, organized, and on-brand.
Maintain a polished and professional appearance, representing each brand with enthusiasm and authenticity.
Collaborate closely with your team to share insights, meet sales and engagement goals, and contribute to the overall success of the campaign.
Track daily interactions and results, providing feedback to your team leader to help improve performance and campaign impact.




Participate in regular training sessions to stay up to date on product knowledge, sales techniques, and brand messaging.




What We’re Looking For

Strong communication and presentation skills
Friendly, confident, and professional demeanour
Background in promotions, hospitality, or customer service is a plus
Able to work flexible, in-person shifts




Why Join Us

Uncapped performance-based pay and advancement opportunities - earn $800–$1,000 per week in your first year, with six-figure potential by year two for top performers
Supportive, high-energy culture focused on personal growth
Ongoing training in brand representation and sales techniques




If you love connecting with people and bringing brands to life, this is your chance to shine.

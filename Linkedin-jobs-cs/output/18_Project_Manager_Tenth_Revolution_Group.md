## Full job description

Project Manager – Engineering & Software Integration

Location: Markham, ON (Fully Onsite)

Job Type: Full‑Time




About the Company:

We are an award‑winning technology firm recognized as one of the Best Places to Work, known for our collaborative culture, employee‑first mindset, and commitment to continuous innovation. The organization is experiencing exciting, sustained growth and offers extensive opportunities for upward mobility, professional development, and career advancement.




Our team specializes in delivering software solutions that bridge engineering workflows with enterprise systems for manufacturing clients across North America. We partner with major leaders in engineering and manufacturing technology and are continuing to expand our footprint rapidly.




Position Summary

We are seeking a Project Manager / Project Engineer to lead software implementation projects from initial scoping through successful go‑live. In this role, you will work directly with customers to understand their engineering processes, gather technical requirements, collaborate with internal software engineers, and manage project execution end‑to‑end.




You’ll join a dynamic team of energetic professionals—project engineers, account managers, and developers—who thrive in a fast‑paced environment and support each other in building world‑class customer experiences.




Key Responsibilities

Manage smooth handoff from sales to implementation and act as the primary project consultant.
Lead customer engagements by scoping requirements, presenting solutions, and delivering implementation services.
Work with internal engineers and customers to determine the best technical solution—or design new enhancements when needed.
Serve as a subject‑matter expert in engineering processes for both clients and technology partners.
Prepare and present project quotations, proposals, and scope adjustments.
Participate in industry events, partner conferences, and occasional onsite customer visits (primarily within the U.S.).
Maintain strong customer relationships by providing ongoing support post‑implementation.
Evaluate engineering workflows and recommend best practices to maximize customer success.




Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical, Electrical, Computer Engineering, or equivalent experience.
Strong passion for understanding customer operations and matching solutions to business needs.
Ability to work in a smaller, collaborative team where everyone wears multiple hats.
Excellent attention to detail while balancing deadlines and competing priorities.
Outstanding communication, critical thinking, and troubleshooting skills.
Strong organizational and time‑management abilities for juggling multiple projects.
Enjoys meeting new people and traveling occasionally.
Positive attitude, sense of humor, and appreciation for a healthy work–life balance.

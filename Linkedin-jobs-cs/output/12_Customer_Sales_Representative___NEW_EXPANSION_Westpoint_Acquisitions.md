## Full job description

We have expanded to Toronto — and we’re hiring Customer Sales Representatives!




Westpoint Acquisitions is growing fast across Eastern Canada, and Toronto is our next big move. We’re building a team of Customer Sales Representatives to represent national brands through in-person interactions, pop-up events, brand activations and promotional stands.




As a Customer Sales Representative, you’ll help connect people with credit card products and services that genuinely make a difference. If you enjoy talking to people, hate sitting still, and want to be part of something that’s actually going somewhere, this could be the role for you.

At Westpoint Acquisitions, we believe in growth — for our clients, our campaigns, and most importantly, our people.




What You’ll Be Doing

Represent national brands in retail settings: As a Customer Sales Representative, you’ll be the friendly face of our partners, sharing credit card product details, promotions, and service options in a professional yet approachable way.
Engage with customers and match them with the right solutions: Ask questions, listen actively, and help customers find plans or products that meet their needs — whether that’s saving money, improving convenience, or upgrading their experience.
Deliver exceptional service: Provide clear, accurate information, resolve questions confidently, and ensure every customer walks away with a positive impression.
Drive campaign performance: Collaborate with fellow Customer Sales Representatives to meet daily and weekly goals, track results, and celebrate team success.
Participate in hands-on training and coaching: Learn advanced sales techniques, communication skills, and leadership strategies through ongoing workshops and mentoring.
Support event and campaign setup: Help design engaging displays, maintain a professional environment, and create memorable brand experiences for every visitor.




What We’re Looking For

We’re not hiring robots — we’re looking for motivated people who:

Enjoy face-to-face interaction and can build quick rapport with customers
Have a positive attitude, even on the busiest days
Are reliable, coachable, and open to learning new skills
Can adapt quickly and think on their feet
Are based in Markham and available to work full-time, in person




Experience in customer service, retail, or sales is an asset, but not required. We’ll train the right Customer Sales Representative from the ground up.




What’s In It for You

A real opportunity to grow — we promote from within
Leadership development and mentorship programs
A supportive, team-first culture that values your effort
Weekly pay with bonuses and performance incentives
Average first-year earnings: $800-$1,000 per week
Six-figure potential for top Customer Sales Representatives by Year 2




The Bottom Line

We’re a company built on growth — for our clients, our campaigns, and our people. As a Customer Sales Representative at Westpoint Acquisitions, you’ll be part of a team that rewards initiative, values communication, and helps you build a long-term career in an expanding industry.

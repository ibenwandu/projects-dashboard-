## Full job description

TTM Technologies, Inc. – Publicly Traded US Company, NASDAQ (TTMI) – Top-5 Global Printed Circuit Board Manufacturer

About TTM

TTM Technologies, Inc. is a leading global manufacturer of technology products, including mission systems, radio frequency (“RF”) components, RF microwave/microelectronic assemblies, and technologically advanced printed circuit boards (“PCB”s). TTM stands for time-to-market, representing how TTM's time-critical, one-stop design, engineering and manufacturing services enable customers to reduce the time required to develop new products and bring them to market.

Additional information can be found at www.ttm.com

Summary

The primary responsibilities of this job profile include: Providing outstanding customer service to assigned customer accounts to positively impact the business relationship and increase customer satisfaction. Working with Program Managers, Account Managers, Engineering, Quality, Manufacturing, and assigned (external) customers to facilitate, coordinate and communicate requests for quotes, material planning, order entry, engineering holds, order changes, schedule sharing, order status, requests for RMA’s, and resolution of shipping and accounting issues. Working with blueprints and purchase orders to resolve customer complaints and returns.

Job Description

TTM strives to invest in our team members at all levels – starting on your first day. To support the growth and development of our new hires, this position is eligible for a 90 Day Evaluation that includes a pay increase if key performance indicators are met.

Scope:


Order administration and communication from order quoting to order shipment
Resolving first level product and service problems internally and with the customer directly
Entering and tracking orders through entire business process (from booking to customer receipt)
Understand and maintain database for backlog reconciliation
Address, resolve and communicate customer issues concerning manufacturability of products
Efficiently resolve issues with front end engineering to ensure product releases are accurate and timely
Communicate with manufacturing departments to resolve on time delivery and quality issues
Conduct contract review on customer purchase orders and resolve issues as necessary
Complete error free order entry into site specific systems
Providing and interpreting reports on sales activities and objective measurements
Support quotation activity for repeat orders and quarterly and annual pricing reviews
Support RMA and customer complaint process and follow up with customer

Who We Are

At Kyndryl, we design, build, manage and modernize the mission-critical technology systems that the world depends on every day. So why work at Kyndryl? We are always moving forward – always pushing ourselves to go further in our efforts to build a more equitable, inclusive world for our employees, our customers and our communities.

The Role

As the Director of AI Innovation Hub at Kyndryl, you will lead a transformative initiative to embed Agentic AI across every Kyndryl service, driving innovation and measurable business impact. This strategic leadership role combines technical vision, commercial acumen, and delivery excellence. You’ll build and lead a world-class team of forward-deployed engineers, create cutting-edge demos and customer journeys, and position Kyndryl as an AI-native enterprise.

We’re not just imagining the future of AI — we’re building it. Fast.

Key Responsibilities

Serve as the strategic bridge between advanced AI technology and business transformation.


Define and execute the roadmap for Agentic AI integration across services and platforms.
Oversee development of intelligent agents and frameworks using Python, Azure ML, and other cutting-edge tools.
Champion rapid prototyping and demo cycles to accelerate adoption and client success.
Collaborate with sales and delivery teams to ensure AI solutions drive tangible business value.
Foster collaboration with academia and research communities, supervising industrial PhDs and contributing to publications.


If you're ready to embrace the power of AI to transform our business and embark on an epic adventure, then join us at Kyndryl. Together, let's redefine what's possible and unleash your potential.

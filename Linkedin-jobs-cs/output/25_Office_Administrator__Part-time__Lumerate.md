## Full job description

Lumerate is growing rapidly and we’re actively hiring for a part time Office Administrator to join our team!

Who is Lumerate?

We help our customers achieve the full picture of their industries. We also strive to achieve our own personal full pictures from a career fulfillment and learning perspective. We’re in the business of gathering intelligence about industries and delivering it to the right people within those industries through innovative software interfaces. Our vision is to be the world's most useful and trusted source of information for professionals seeking to understand what's happening in their industry. Our mission: To deliver industry awareness to an ever-increasing number of people, in whatever way helps them to make the most informed decisions, take the most immediate action and be the most awesome at their unique jobs.

What The Role Looks Like

We are a thriving hybrid team in the office on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays at our office located in the Junction Triangle. Our Office Administrator will play a key role in keeping things running smoothly, supporting both in‑office and remote team members, and managing day-to-day operations. This role will work closely with our Talent & Culture and Finance & Operations team.

Other Day To Day Responsibilities Include


Supporting the day-to-day operations of the office by coordinating logistics, managing supplies, and ensuring the workplace runs smoothly for the team
Preparing desks, equipment, and welcome packages for new employees and coordinating onboarding and offboarding logistics
Managing office resources including, computer, supplies, and inventory.
Coordinating building maintenance requests and working with vendors or building management to resolve issues
Tracking key office resources such as stipends, parking, and shared office equipment
Planning and coordinating monthly in-office events
Maintaining the office emergency plan, organizing fire drills, and meeting all health and safety requirements

Friendly and approachable, and naturally enjoys creating a welcoming and positive environment for others
Highly organized and comfortable managing multiple moving pieces, trackers, and logistical details

Why Lumerate?


Gain hands-on experience supporting the day-to-day operations of a growing company
Work with people from a variety of departments including Customer Acquisition, Data, Customer Success and others.
Play a meaningful role in shaping the day-to-day employee experience and office environment

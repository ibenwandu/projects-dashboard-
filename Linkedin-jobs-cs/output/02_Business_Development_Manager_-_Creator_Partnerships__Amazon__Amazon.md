## Full job description

Description

Amazon Canada is seeking a talented, highly motivated and experienced Business Development Manager to be part of our Canadian Creator Business team. The role is based in Toronto, and works out of our downtown Toronto Tech Hub 5 days per week.

The Amazon Canada Creators Business Development Team collaborates with a large number of Canadian content creators including websites, publishers, and Influencers. These Creators create original content to help customers make informed purchase decisions on Amazon. Our Creators business is an integral part of the Amazon marketing mix, bringing new customers to the site, driving existing customers to discover new products or programs including the many benefits of Amazon Prime. With its global network of over 1MM publishers and influencers, the Creators program is a leader in online marketing and one of the largest paid marketing channels for Amazon.

As a customer obsessed, digital media enthusiast, you will influence and build upon the success we have had with top Canadian Publishers and Influencers. Using your deep knowledge of the industry and digital media landscape, and with a solution-oriented mindset, you will identify and develop new business relationships and opportunities for Amazon and collaborate with and influence our category, deal and product teams to develop innovative programs which help unlock new opportunities for Amazon and our Creators.

You have experience working in technical businesses and have the ability to influence technical leaders and incorporate technical requirements into negotiations and contracts.

You are an excellent oral and written communicator, and you possess an analytical approach to business development, leveraging data to drive decisions.

You are highly organized and have the ability to work closely and constructively with multiple internal and external stakeholders. You excel at developing relationships – both internal and external. This role will work closely with many of Amazon’s strategic businesses to structure innovative and impactful deals.

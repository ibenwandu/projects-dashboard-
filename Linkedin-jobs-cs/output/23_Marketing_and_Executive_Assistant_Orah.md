## Full job description

## About the Role




Orah is looking for a high-energy, deeply organised Marketing & Executive Assistant who thrives on making things happen. This role sits at the heart of our Marketing team — keeping the machine running, the executives moving, and the details locked down.




You'll work directly for our Head of Marketing, with regular support across Sales, the CEO, and Customer Success teams. Think of yourself as the operational backbone of our go-to-market motion: the person who gets things done before anyone even has to ask twice.




If you love a full plate, take serious pride in doing things properly, and genuinely enjoy the craft of organisation — this role is made for you.




---




## What You'll Do




**Travel & Logistics**




- Plan and book all travel for the marketing and sales team — flights, accommodation, ground transport, and itineraries

- Anticipate logistics needs and handle changes without fuss

- Keep travel costs sensible while keeping the experience smooth




**Marketing Campaign Support**




- Assist with the execution of marketing campaigns — uploading content, scheduling sends, and keeping campaigns on track

- Proofread marketing materials, emails, and assets before they go out the door

- Draft email campaigns and marketing copy for review, with a good eye for tone and detail

- Coordinate with the Sales team to ensure leads generated from campaigns are properly tracked and followed up on

- Help maintain marketing tools and CRM hygiene so nothing falls through the cracks




**Events, Community & Hospitality**




- Research and secure reservations for corporate dinners, team events, and client entertainment

- Know how to find the right venue for the right moment — a closing dinner feels different to a team lunch

- Coordinate event logistics end-to-end, from invitations to on-the-day execution

- Coordinate Orah's presence at education conferences and trade shows — booth logistics, materials, swag packs, and staffing schedules

- Manage event registrations, attendee communications, and follow-ups

- Source and book venues for hosted events such as webinars, roundtables, and customer meetups

- Help coordinate customer advisory calls and roundtable dinners, managing invite lists and RSVPs for exclusive customer events

- Send follow-up gifts or personal touches after key customer interactions

- Liaise with event sponsors and co-marketing partners on logistics and deliverables, tracking commitments on both sides

- Monitor and engage in relevant online communities (LinkedIn groups, edtech forums) on behalf of the Orah brand

- Support webinar logistics — registrations, reminders, and post-event follow-up sequences

- Coordinate repurposing of event content for social media in collaboration with the wider marketing team




**Swag, Gifts & Procurement**




- Source and manage branded swag, customer gifts, and team rewards

- Manage vendor relationships, track orders, and ensure timely delivery

- Keep inventory organised and replenishment proactive, not reactive




**General Team Operations**




- Handle ad hoc tasks and projects across the go-to-market team with high ownership and low drama

- Keep shared tools, trackers, and documents up to date

- Be the kind of person the team relies on — and knows they can

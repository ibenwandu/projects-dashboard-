🛠️ What You’ll Do


Coordinate and support Maintenance administrative duties  
Open and maintain company work orders and task cards in the Maintenance, Repair, and Operations (MRO) Software System. 
Ensure all task cards, log pages, and work packages are completed, accounted for, and digitized prior to filing to the appropriate aircraft archives 
Ensure that maintenance Work Orders balance with Accounting Department records 
Track Maintenance Time & Attendance, administrating the system 
Input timesheets and labour time 
Administrative Support 


🎯  What You Bring: 


College Certificate or Diploma in Business Administration or related courses  
Minimum of 2 years of related administrative experience 
Knowledge of Microsoft Office (Excel & Word) 
Aviation industry experience will be considered an asset 
Solid verbal and written communication skills 
Ability to prioritize and work in a fast-paced environment 
Capacity to influence and demonstrate interpersonal skills

## Full job description

Being part of Air Canada is to become part of an iconic Canadian symbol, recently ranked the best Airline in North America. Let your career take flight by joining our diverse and vibrant team at the leading edge of passenger aviation.

The Resource Scheduling Support Analyst is responsible for operational and cost objective metrics through efficient and strategic resource scheduling.

Responsibilities


Reconcile headcount base salaries along with overtime and under time payments
Develop and execute the staffing bids for assigned station/region
Manage long term vacancies and ensure staff is allocated
Liaise with union representatives to address employee concerns
Manage vacation levels and subsequent backfill
Execute equalization of staff schedules at the completion of a staffing bid
Delivers advanced planning of staffing levels a month in advance in response to operational changes of the monthly headcount targets while controlling costs
Prepare documentation & reporting of schedule assignments for payroll purposes
Responsible for tracking and coordination of staff hiring, of staff movement and/or employee status change
Manage rolling employee seniority lists resulting from retirements, resignations, transfers, and terminations
Communicate schedules and assignments directly to employees
Monthly payroll/headcount audit
Manage escalation of employee pay concerns
Ensure that risks related to Safety and Security are addressed
Responsible for supporting the safety of operations within their assigned region
Supports the audits of Air Canada SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures). Ensures compliance with standards of IOSA, SMS and other applicable authorities.
Responsible for supporting Management for safety, security, and customer service activities within responsible region
Anticipates and identifies safety, security, service, and operational problems, and resolves them with the branch safety team while maintaining the focus on safety
Development of daily staff rosters
Control of time off incurred with shift cycle changes, assignments ensuring appropriate payroll advise
Responsible for processing/approving requests from union office for union time clearance
Prepares all work plan related documents for distribution to unionized employees
Responsible to coordinate with employee reliability on all rehabilitation assignments and return to work changes
Develops and deploys plan to achieve mandatory and regulatory training of unionized employees including completion and compliance


Qualifications


College Certificate or Diploma in Human Resources (bachelor's an asset)
Commitment to employee engagement
High energy with solid planning skills
Proven time management
Ability to establish work priorities and perform while under tight deadlines
Demonstrated strong interpersonal skills
Excellent communication skills, verbal and written
Advanced knowledge of MS Office
Ability to work under pressure, meet deadlines and adapt to changing conditions
Must be a self-starter and be able to work under minimal supervision
Experience working in a unionized environment an asset; thorough knowledge of IAMAW or CAW collective agreements
Ability to work on a rotating shift pattern including weekends and the possibility of some extended hours
Compliance with standards of IOSA, SMS and other applicable authorities
Demonstrate punctuality and dependability to support overall team success in a fast-paced environment

Position Summary

As a Junior Software Quality Assurance Analyst at Premier Construction Software, you’ll begin your career in a dynamic, fast-paced environment focused on delivering high-quality, cloud-based ERP solutions for the construction industry. Reporting to the QA Manager, you’ll collaborate with experienced QA professionals, developers, and support teams to ensure the reliability and performance of our platform.

This entry-level role is designed to build your foundation in software testing, defect tracking, and process improvement—preparing you for long-term growth in quality assurance or software development.

Key Responsibilities


Design, execute, and maintain manual and automated test cases to validate business requirements and software functionality.
Identify, document, and track software defects using industry-standard tools; work closely with developers to investigate and resolve issues.
Analyze system specifications and user stories to determine testing scope and strategy.
Validate accounting and project management reports for accuracy and compliance with business logic.
Assist in expanding and maintaining automated test suites under the guidance of senior QA engineers.
Collaborate with support and development teams to triage and prioritize incoming support cases.
Document test results, defects, and testing phases in issue tracking systems.
Participate in agile ceremonies, team meetings, and product demos to gain full lifecycle exposure.


Qualifications


Bachelor’s degree (or final-year student) in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or a related field.
Foundational knowledge of software testing methodologies and QA processes.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with high attention to detail.
Effective communication skills and a collaborative mindset.
Familiarity with databases and basic SQL is an asset.
Exposure to web/cloud-based applications or accounting software is a plus.
Self-driven, eager to learn, and capable of managing time effectively to meet deadlines.


Growth & Learning Opportunities


Develop hands-on experience in manual and automated testing.
Learn to translate business requirements into effective test plans.
Gain exposure to defect tracking, documentation, and cross-functional collaboration.
Understand the role of QA in the software development lifecycle.
Build proficiency with QA tools such as issue tracking systems and test automation frameworks.


Additional Information


Flexible work hours and duration based on business needs and candidate availability.
Mentorship from experienced QA professionals with regular feedback and coaching.
Opportunity to contribute to real projects and make a measurable impact on product quality.

## Full job description

Job Overview

We are looking for a highly organized and proactive Business Support / Business Analyst to support the Customer Protection team at Bank. This role involves supporting day-to-day business operations, assisting with presentations and documentation, and collaborating with cross-functional and technical teams.

The ideal candidate is detail-oriented, comfortable working with data and presentations, and able to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment.

Key Responsibilities

Provide business and operational support to the team on day-to-day activities.
Prepare presentations, slide decks, reports, and written documentation for internal stakeholders.
Assist with requirements gathering and documentation for business initiatives.
Work with Excel and spreadsheets to manage, organize, and analyze data.
Support cross-functional teams and collaborate with both business and technical stakeholders.
Coordinate with technical teams to support business needs and ensure clear communication.
Assist with additional workload and operational tasks as needed.
Maintain strong organization and prioritization to manage multiple tasks effectively.

Required Qualifications

5+ years of experience in a business support, operations support, or business analyst role.
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with a positive and collaborative attitude.
Strong organizational and multitasking abilities.
Proven experience creating presentations, slide decks, and written business materials.
Strong proficiency in MS Excel and spreadsheets.
Experience providing cross-functional support across teams.
Ability to work closely with technical teams and understand technical concepts.
North American education background preferred.
Must be comfortable working in-office and engaging with team members regularly.

Nice to Have

Prior experience working with Bank.

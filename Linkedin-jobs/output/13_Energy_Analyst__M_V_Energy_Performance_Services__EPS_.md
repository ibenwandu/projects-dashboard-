(Measurement & Verification) 

 

INSPIRING ORGANIZATIONS TO MANAGE ENERGY BETTER!  

Would you like to join an organization that delivers energy improvement services and that is driven by a passion to contribute to environmental solutions that will make our planet a better place?  




If yes, this position is for YOU!  




Energy Performance Services (EPS) Inc. (“EPS”), incorporated in 1992, has specialized in industrial energy management for over 30 years. EPS works with large energy using industrial organizations in North America to establish continuous improvement systems for energy, by focusing on better utilizing the systems and equipment that are already in place. Forms of energy management that EPS specializes in include Strategic Energy Management (SEM) programs, Energy Management Information Systems (EMIS), and Energy Management Systems such as ISO 50001.  

For more information, please visit our website at www.epsenergie.com.   




JOB SUMMARY 

As part of the Measurement and Verification (M&V) team at EPS, your role will be to support the delivery of various energy management programs to multiple large industrial clients. The focus of this role will be to actively participate in the production of high-quality M&V Plans, M&V Reports and support the approval process with the programs and evaluators. 




Specific tasks of the M&V Energy Analyst will include: 

Evaluating energy performance for various industrial energy conservation projects using multivariate regression models and/or other engineering calculations conforming to recognized M&V protocols. 
Drafting and/or reviewing M&V Plans and M&V reports 
Communicating energy performance with clients on a regular basis  
Developing insights into energy savings within industrial facilities to identify potential risks and or opportunities in maintaining performance over time 
Providing technical support to internal team members or external clients in finding, validating, developing, and implementing energy savings opportunities in industrial systems (compressors, boilers, chillers, HVAC, pumps, fans, major process equipment, etc.) 
Where necessary, obtaining metered data within industrial networks (equipment, PLCs, HMIs, data historians, etc.) to measure performance improvements 
Interaction with the clients mainly via teleconferencing and occasionally in person and on site   
Participating in on-site visits to establish customer relationships, process understanding or setting up measurement equipment 
Supporting and/or leading junior Energy Analysts in generating viable M&V strategies, performing engineering calculations and analyzing complex systems 
Deliver energy savings

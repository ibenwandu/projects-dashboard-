## Full job description

About the Opportunity

Prepare weekly and monthly reporting, such as Sales reports and customer P&L
Identify areas of opportunity and/ or concern and provide recommendations
Prepare weekly variance analysis, including sales and GP reporting, with a high degree of accuracy and strict adherence to deadlines
Manage and review general accounting journal entries
Ensure that general accounting journal entries are accurately recorded in accordance with company deadlines and accounting processes
Communicate with field operations to accurately process Commissary & Non-Trade Business
Support the annual budget and forecasting initiatives
Other tasks that will be assigned on an ad hoc basis




About You

Minimum of 2 years of experience in an accounting or financial analysis role
Currently pursuing a CPA designation
Experience with month-end duties, including accruals and journal entries
Computer savvy and proficient in Microsoft Suite, i.e. Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook
Ability to work independently and carry out assignments with minimal instruction
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
Macro programming skills are an asset

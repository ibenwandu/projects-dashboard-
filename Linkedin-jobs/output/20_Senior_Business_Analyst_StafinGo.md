## Full job description

Hiring: Senior Business Analyst

Location: Toronto, Onsite (5 days a week in office)

Duration: April 1, 2026 – March 31, 2027 (possibility to extend)

Hourly Rate: $75/hour - $90/hour (based on experience)

Client: Public Sector - Healthcare




Responsibilities:

Work with lines of Agency assets such as Provincial Care Coordination Product, Provincial Health Service Directory Product (business, architects, developers, UI and UX designers to collect and analyze requirements (i.e. business, integration, interface, data, and non-functional requirements) for new builds, enhancements)
Work with both Central Intake vendors and Agency asset product owners to create/update/refine user stories, leverage techniques to drive out business priorities and maintain backlog.
Contribute to agile sprint reviews, product demos and retrospectives.
Document business and system solutions using Agile methodology techniques, as well as support the development of test scripts for end to end functional testing.
Co-ordination and facilitation of stakeholder meetings, with clear agendas, minutes and follow up.
Assist with project initiation and governance by preparing necessary documents and reports.
Help identify risks, assumptions and constraints for requirements, and communicate to internal and external stakeholders.
Ensure performance and customer experience metrics are defined, measured and reported on a regular basis.
Work with project manager to support continuous improvement of processes to optimize team productivity, quality and time-to-market.
Manage multiple tasks, work assignments simultaneously, and meet delivery dates, while working under pressure and tight deadlines.

7+ years working experience as a Business Analyst on health care/IT projects

7+ years Document business and system solutions using Agile methodology techniques, as well as support the development of test scripts for end to end functional testing.

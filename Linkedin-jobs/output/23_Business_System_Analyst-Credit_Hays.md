## Full job description

Role: Business Systems Analyst – Credit (with Data & System Analysis)

Rate: $50-$60/hr

About the Role

We are seeking an experienced Business Systems Analyst (BSA) with strong expertise in Credit systems, data analysis, and system workflow analysis. This role will support credit operations, lending technology teams, and business stakeholders by translating requirements into functional specifications and ensuring smooth delivery of credit-related initiatives.

If you have a strong understanding of credit processes, data flows, and systems integration within lending environments, we’d love to hear from you.

Key Responsibilities

Collaborate with credit, risk, operations, and technology teams to gather and document business and functional requirements.
Analyze current-state system architecture, data flows, and integration points across lending and credit platforms.
Conduct gap analysis to support enhancements, automation, or new credit product launches.
Perform data analysis using SQL, Excel, and reporting tools to validate data mapping, lineage, and accuracy.
Partner with developers, QA teams, and architects to ensure clear communication of business needs.
Support testing activities including test case development, UAT execution, and defect triage.
Prepare documentation such as BRDs, FRDs, process flows, data flow diagrams, and user guides.
Participate in Agile ceremonies including daily stand-ups, grooming sessions, and sprint planning.
Provide production support for credit systems, resolving issues related to data, workflow, or system behavior.

Required Skills & Experience

5–8+ years of experience as a Business Systems Analyst, preferably in banking, lending, or financial services.
Strong domain knowledge in Credit, underwriting workflows, decisioning processes, and lending operations.
Hands-on data analysis skills including SQL, data profiling, data mapping, and reconciliation.
Understanding of workflow tools, rules engines, and credit decisioning platforms.
Excellent communication skills with the ability to liaise between business and technical teams.
Experience working in Agile environments (Scrum preferred).

Scope

The Senior Financial Data & BI Analyst is responsible for consolidating and standardizing financial and operational data from multiple enterprise systems to deliver accurate, reconciled, and trusted business intelligence outputs. This role partners closely with Finance and cross-functional teams to ensure data integrity, consistency, and alignment with financial records. This role will consolidate data across two ERPs, a CRM, logistics systems, and spreadsheets, and build accurate, reconciled, and standardized reporting outputs. The position will own the process of investigating source systems, designing standardization rules, validating data against GL, billing, and revenue recognition, and delivering trustworthy BI assets.

Duties & Responsibilities


Data Discovery & Extraction (SQL Server)
Analyze SQL Server database schemas to understand the data structure and relationships
Write advanced SQL queries (joins, CTEs, aggregations) to extract financial and operational data from ERP database tables
Design and build SQL scripts that extract, validate, and reconcile ERP transaction data (including revenue, sales, and GL postings) into standardized datasets used for period-end close and audit procedures
Create staging tables, mapping tables, and QA datasets for reconciliation.
Reconciliation & Financial Validation
Perform reconciliations against:
General Ledger (GL)
Billing systems
Revenue recognition outputs
Perform variance analysis, completeness checks, tie-outs, and exception tracking.
Document reconciliation logic and provide audit-ready evidence.
Standardization & Rule-Building
Build a global standardized category model that unifies inconsistent ERP, CRM, logistics, and spreadsheet data
Develop rulesets (conditional logic) to categorize non-uniform data into predefined categories
Create and maintain mapping tables; ensure rules are transparent, testable, and repeatable
Power BI Reporting & Data Modeling
Build semantic models, DAX measures, dataflows, and dashboards in Power BI
Implement refresh pipelines and monitor data quality
Deliver intuitive reports with drill-downs, filters, and standardized KPI logic
Documentation & Collaboration
Maintain complete documentation: definitions, mappings, data lineage, QA processes
Collaborate closely with Finance, Ops, Sales, and IT teams to refine requirements
Present reconciliation outcomes and ensure stakeholder confidence in final report

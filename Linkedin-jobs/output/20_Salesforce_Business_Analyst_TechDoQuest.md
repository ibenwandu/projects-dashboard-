## Full job description

Summary:




We are looking for a Salesforce Business Analyst with strong experience in Financial Services to act as the functional bridge between business stakeholders and the Salesforce technical team. The role focuses on delivering scalable, compliant, and value-driven solutions in a regulated financial environment.




Roles & Responsibilities:




Translate high‑level business requirements into detailed user stories with clear acceptance criteria.
Drive stakeholder workshops and ensure alignment between business needs and technical delivery.
Collaborate with technical teams to design scalable solutions using Salesforce features (custom objects, flows, reports).
Lead User Acceptance Testing (UAT), regression testing (including seasonal releases), and defect tracking via Jira.
Identify, document, and track issues to ensure production stability and release readiness.
Develop training materials and support user adoption across the platform.




Qualifications & Skills:




Mandatory: Salesforce Business Analyst
Mandatory: Experience in Financial Services or similar industries
Good to Have: Salesforce Financial Services Cloud (FSC) certification

Job Title: Technical Business Analyst

Location: Mississauga – Hybrid – 3 days/week

Term: Until Sep 30th contract plus extension




Role is a support role not a SME PM

Role is mix of PM and Business/ Data Analyst

Must have strong organizational skills

Must have project management experience

Able to manage meetings/minutes, put together decks, pull data, build a tracker, manage overlapping deliveries

Must have rates and trading experience

Profile around 5 to 8 yrs experience – ideally in Rates Markets Business / Rates Data Governance (Rates Ops / Middle Office background helps)

- Good understanding of Rates Front to Back Trade lifecycle.

- Will be required to work on both strategic and ad hoc initiatives.

- Comfortable performing support role -Tracking progress across Consent Order use cases, putting together Data Governance Slide / Decks, Meeting minutes, etc.

- Proactive, good communicator, self-starter, strong attention to detail.




Key Responsibilities:

• Ability to build collaborative relationships across Global Markets front-to-back stakeholders, e.g., trading desk, finance, operations, technology, data stewards, risk management.

• Produce accurate project update materials and artifacts, tailoring them to various forums and committees.




Key Skills Required:

• 2-8 years of relevant industry experience, ideally in Investment Bank.

• Strong experience in Data Governance / Project Management / Data Analysis.

• Must be an energetic self-starter/able to take initiative and work effectively with guidance.

• Familiarity with Investment Bank / Capital Markets operations.

• Good understanding of Rates derivatives, exchange traded products, trading workflows, and the Trade Lifecycle.

• Working knowledge of Data governance practices: Data lineage, Data quality, metadata management, data controls.

• Proficiency in SQL, with Python knowledge being an advantage, for data analysis and visualization.

• Strong oral and written communication skills; able to articulate information clearly and concisely.

• Ability to collaborate effectively with stakeholders and contribute to achieving consensus.

• Ability to support change initiatives to business practices by working effectively across a global organization and understanding different perspectives.

• Demonstrate analytical skills with follow-up and problem-solving capability.

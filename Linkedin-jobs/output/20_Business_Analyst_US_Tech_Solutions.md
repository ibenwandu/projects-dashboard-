## Full job description

Work Model: Hybrid – 4 days onsite

Duration: 1 Year




About the role:

Our client is seeking Business Analyst V professionals to support one of their largest migration projects—transitioning one book of record to a new platform. This role requires someone who can work independently, host partner discussions, document current and future state requirements, and proactively propose solutions to support conversion activities.




Responsibilities

Must-Have Responsibilities:

Facilitate meetings with internal partners to gather and document business requirements
Conduct detailed current-state analysis and define future-state requirements
Support conversion activities through thorough documentation and proactive recommendations
Host calls and lead requirement-gathering discussions independently
Create detailed process maps to support migration and operational readiness

Experience

Ideally 10 years of progressive Business Analyst experience (flexible with strong skills)
Experience in investments such as mutual funds, registered products, CIRO regulations, or Fundserv is highly beneficial
Previous client experience is an asset
Proven experience working on migration or large-scale transformation projects




Skillsets

Must-Have Skills:

Advanced PowerPoint expertise
Strong process mapping capabilities

Soft Skills:

Nice-to-Have Skills:

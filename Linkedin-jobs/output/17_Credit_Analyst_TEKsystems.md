Description

Key Responsibilities:


Prepare timely and accurate credit applications for renewals, annual reviews, and ad‑hoc monitoring by conducting detailed assessments of borrower creditworthiness, including analysis of



financial statements, financial modeling (Base and Stress scenarios), industry conditions, economic trends, emerging risks, and overall credit risk.


Promptly respond to inquiries and requests from the Credit Department to support efficient review processes and facilitate timely credit approvals.
Apply sound credit judgment by demonstrating a solid understanding of end‑to‑end credit application processes while ensuring full adherence to credit policies, procedures, and regulatory



requirements.


Monitor credit‑related trigger events, including customer financial disclosures and press releasedpromptly notifying 1LoD Management of developments requiring attention. File Credit Alarms as



required by procedures.


Prepare accurate periodic reports on borrower financial position (including Watchlist and Leverage Lending), credit facilities, exposures, and related metrics, and distribute them to the Management as required.
Collaborate with internal First Line of Defense teams to update and maintain credit facility data in the Bank’s operating systems (e.g., GBR and LIQ).
Support the Department Head by assisting with special projects as assigned.

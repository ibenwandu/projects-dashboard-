## Full job description

Career Opportunity

Role Title

Business Analyst

Purpose of role

Reporting to the Senior Manager, Business Analysis – IT; the Business Analyst is responsible for participating in the evaluation of the impact of critical and strategic business solutions by applying industry and Foresters Financial-specific knowledge and industry best practices in product/system/process development, business process design/re-design, and systems analysis and design. The Business Analyst is also responsible for assisting in the development and implementation of the critical and strategic business solutions.


Job Description

Key Responsibilities


Participate in the evaluation of the impact of business solutions by applying industry and Foresters Financial specific knowledge and industry best practices on product/system/process development life cycle, business process design/re-design, system analysis and design and project management
Deliver and communicate critical business solutions in a project environment through analyzing and developing business requirements and design specifications; analyzing and developing business process designs/re-designs; contributing to test criteria definition and problem resolution; and developing training requirements
Provide support to Foresters Financial strategies and ongoing operations by contributing to the identification of gaps and overlaps between projects, business processes and ongoing operations; ensuring the integration and links between projects and ongoing operations; and defining product/process rules and parameters to application systems
Build efficient and effective relationships with external vendors of services through efficient performance of business specification development, contribution to quality assurance testing, and contribution to the management of costs associated with software changes
Adhere to Foresters Financial security standards and procedures during solution implementation and support
Report security incidents as per the Foresters Financial Information Security Incident Management Standard



Key Qualifications


Post secondary education preferred and/or 3-5 years' experience with insurance product knowledge and systems
Ability to work effectively with a wide variety of tools (e.g. LucidCharts, Copilot Studio, Azure DevOps, Microsoft Office including Excel, Word, Access and Project, APL, TSO) as well as have an understanding of the technology environment (e.g. Client Server) within which the tools reside
Familiar with the life cycle of product, process, and system development, as well as the concepts and practices
Ability to assess system capabilities, impact on business processes, recommend system designs and incorporate activities into all development life cycle phases
Possess an understanding of Foresters Financial strategic direction, vision and goals as well as Foresters products, insurance regulations, business process design re-design methodologies, business processes and procedures and supporting application systems (functionality, integration/flow, file structures/tables etc.)
Strong analytical, reasoning and problem-solving abilities
Must be able to identify problems and recommend business solutions
Ability to plan, organize and coordinate tasks
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
Strong interpersonal skills, including the ability to build relationships
Desire and willingness to invest in the growth of your career within the Business Analyst role

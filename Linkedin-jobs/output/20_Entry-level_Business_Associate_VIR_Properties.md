You will work closely with experienced team members to support business development initiatives and contribute to the success of marketing campaigns. Conduct market research and competitor analysis
Maintain accurate records of campaign performance and customer interactions
Collaborate with marketing and sales teams to achieve campaign objectives
Support internal coordination and project tracking
Contribute ideas to improve campaign performance and business growth

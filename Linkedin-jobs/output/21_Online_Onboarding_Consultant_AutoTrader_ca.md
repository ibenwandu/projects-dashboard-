This role will be within the AutoSync team. AutoSync supports over 3,500 dealerships nationwide, delivering cutting-edge software tools to simplify operations, boost marketing, and drive sales.

Online Onboarding Consultant ( 6 months Temp- FT)

The Online Onboarding Consultant is responsible for the implementation and integrations with a focus on new customer onboarding. Each Consultant is responsible for the successful launch and delivery of our Software and Marketplace products. The Onboarding Consultants will work closely with Project Managers and other internal teams to ensure timely delivery.

Their primary responsibilities include configuration of inventory and customer data files, setting up integrations with third parties, and configuring all software settings. They are responsible for each component of the software configuration and level of quality. The incumbent must manage each task effectively to meet expected project timelines for the customer’s onboarding experience.

The Online Onboarding Consultant will be functional across products that include Websites, CRM, Digital Retailing, Desking, and Marketplace.

Key Areas of Responsibility


Assist clients and Project Managers in the initial setup and installation of the software. Configure settings that align with product requirements and workflows.
Ensure data migration of inventory and/or customer files is seamless and accurate.
Collaborate with management, development, and project teams to resolve complex problems or issues that arise during the onboarding process.
Build and maintain strong relationships internally and with clients to ensure satisfaction and retention.

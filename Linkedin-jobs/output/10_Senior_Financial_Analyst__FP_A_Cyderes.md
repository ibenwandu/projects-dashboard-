Role Summary

Cyderes is seeking a proactive Senior Financial Analyst to join our Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) team. This role provides a unique opportunity to partner with functional leaders across the organization, influence planning and performance discussions, and deliver clear financial insights that drive business decisions.

You will work in a dynamic, fast-paced environment where priorities evolve quickly and financial clarity is crucial. The successful candidate will be comfortable translating complex financial information into actionable guidance, developing and improving reporting processes, and working collaboratively with both finance and non-finance stakeholders.

Responsibilities


Finance Business Partnering & Decision Support
Partner with leaders across functional areas to support financial planning, performance tracking, and decision-making
Translate financial data into clear insights and explain key performance drivers to non-financial stakeholders
Prepare and present financial analysis and results to senior leadership
Forecasting, Budgeting & Financial Management
Support the annual budgeting process and periodic forecasting cycles
Monitor operating metrics, expense drivers, and revenue performance relative to plan
Contribute to headcount and expense modeling as part of long-range planning
Reporting & Financial Analysis
Develop, maintain, and distribute periodic financial reporting packages
Conduct ad-hoc financial analysis to answer business questions and support operational initiatives
Build financial models to support planning, forecasting, and scenario analysis
Process Improvement & Best Practices
Identify and implement improvements to financial reporting, forecasting, and analysis processes
Support automation efforts and adoption of best practices within finance
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure data integrity and timely reporting
Special Projects
Participate in finance-led special projects, including system enhancements, reporting transitions, and business process documentation
Support business cases and financial impact analysis for strategic initiatives





Requirements


3–6 years of progressive experience in financial planning & analysis, corporate finance, accounting, or related areas
Strong analytical, financial modeling, and problem-solving skills
Ability to communicate complex financial information clearly to non-financial stakeholders
Advanced Excel skills and proficiency with financial systems
Comfortable working in a dynamic environment with evolving priorities
CPA designation (completed or in progress)
Experience in high-growth, technology, SaaS, or services organizations
Exposure to financial process redesign or reporting improvements

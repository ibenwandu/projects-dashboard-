The opportunity

The Senior Business Systems Analyst (Sr. BSA) plays an integral role in the delivery of high-quality solutions that meet our clients’ vision and objectives. The ideal candidate is a passionate, highly analytical, personable, and goal-oriented individual who seeks to make significant positive contributions to the firm.

What you will do:


Work closely with business stakeholders to gain an understanding of their needs and to translate their needs to solution requirements
Complete required BSA deliverables within set timelines.
Activities and deliverables may include:
Conducting business process mapping, including live mapping sessions with business stakeholders
Documenting solution requirements in the form of functional and non-functional documents
Assisting with the creation of vendor evaluation documents for procurement purposes
Creating custom documents to meet ad hoc requests, e.g., governance process documents, disaster recovery plans, etc.
Documenting data mapping and conversion rules for data migration projects
Communicate requirements to interested parties e.g., technical and business stakeholders
Mentor business systems analysts and other employees who are interested in learning more about the role
Review profiles of assigned projects to determine BSA scope of work and estimated work effort
Contribute to developing and maintaining processes, procedures, standards, and tools for the BSA service

Your qualifications:


5 - 7 years of experience in a business analyst or business systems analyst role
Extensive experience with business process mapping, process re-engineering and modeling
Strong analytical, problem-solving, and time management skills
Exceptional interpersonal, communication, facilitation, and documentation skills, including the ability to create concise requirements and easy-to-follow process flow diagrams
Ability to develop strong working relationships and to communicate effectively with business and IT stakeholders, and to forge consensus within a group of stakeholders, to deliver a solution that works for all
Knowledge of agile methodology and the software development life cycle (SDLC)
Familiarity with a project management environment dealing with end-user and infrastructure systems in a team-based environment with situations involving rapid change, shifting priorities and/or simultaneous demands

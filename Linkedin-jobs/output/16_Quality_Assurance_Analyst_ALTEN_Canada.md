We are looking to hire a Quality Assurance Analyst for our client in Toronto.




Must Haves:

A minimum of 3 years of experience in the field of software quality assurance or related software development lifecycle activities.
Proven experience in software testing and quality assurance, including test planning, test execution, and defect management.
Proficiency in manual and automated testing techniques and tools (e.g. Copado Robotic Testing, Selenium, etc.).
Ability to write and maintain automated test scripts.
Familiarity with defect tracking systems and test management tools (e.g. Azure DevOps Test Plans)
Any of the following certifications:
Certified Software Testing Engineer (CSTE)
Certified Associate in Software Testing (CAST)
Certified Tester Foundation Level (CTFL)
Certified Software Quality Analyst (CSQA)

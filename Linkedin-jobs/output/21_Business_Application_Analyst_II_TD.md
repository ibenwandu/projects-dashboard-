Job Description

Department Overview

TD AI Platform supports the rollout and sustainment of enterprise AI capabilities by providing standardized, well-governed, and scalable operational support across infrastructure, applications, and services.

Role Summary

As a Level 7 Business Application Analyst, you will be responsible for executing day-to-day business application management and operational support activities for the TD AI Platform. The role is primarily execution-focused, supporting multiple applications and services while ensuring operational stability, documentation quality, and effective coordination with business and technology partners.

Key Accountabilities

Customer


Execute day-to-day operational and administrative support for multiple applications owned and managed by the team, including access management.
Provide ongoing application support to business partners and users, ensuring issues and requests are addressed in a timely manner.
Document and maintain all business-owned artifacts required for projects and major initiatives in accordance with enterprise project delivery and application lifecycle practices.
Support and contribute input to scorecards, dashboards, and operational monitoring metrics for applications.
Manage and maintain Business Operations documentation required to support BAU activities.
Continuously identify opportunities to enhance productivity, effectiveness, and operational efficiency.
Implement enhancements to existing workflows to improve automation, standardization, and overall efficiency.
Support change management activities to ensure project and release changes are transitioned effectively into BAU operations.
Assist with Business Acceptance Testing (BAT) for projects and release maintenance activities.


Shareholder


Adhere to the EPMO project management methodology and established delivery practices.
Ensure compliance with the Data Governance Framework and related enterprise standards.
Build and maintain effective working relationships with team members and key stakeholders.
Continuously enhance knowledge of supported technologies, business domains, and products/services.
Support the development of business cases, including benefits identification, where required.


Employee / Team


Participate fully as a member of the team, contributing to a positive, collaborative, and inclusive work environment.
Support knowledge transfer by continuously enhancing expertise and sharing knowledge within the team and business unit.
Remain current on emerging trends, tools, and techniques relevant to business application management.
Participate in performance management and development activities, including cross-training.
Keep stakeholders informed of the status and progress of assigned work and activities.
Contribute to team success by assisting others and providing guidance or training as appropriate.
Act as a brand ambassador for the business area and TD, both internally and externally.
Participate in cross-functional groups or committees to support broader knowledge sharing.


Breadth & Depth


Professional role focused on providing functional and specialized business application management support within defined parameters.
Requires sound knowledge of supported applications, including system components, interfaces, data flows, and business rules.
Solid conceptual knowledge of practices and procedures within the area of specialty.
Solid analytical and problem-solving skills to investigate issues and recommend solutions.
May lead small initiatives or support workstreams within larger projects.
Executes work of moderate complexity with a short- to medium-term focus.
Work is generally reviewed by a more senior Business Analyst or Manager.


Experience & Education


Undergraduate degree or equivalent practical experience.
Typically 4–7 years of relevant experience in business application support or operations.
Required: JIRA, Confluence.
Nice to have: exposure to cloud platforms, AI initiatives, or data-driven environments.

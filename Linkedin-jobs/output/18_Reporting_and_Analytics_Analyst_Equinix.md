We’re looking for a Reporting & Analytics Analyst to support business decision-making through data exploration, reporting, and insights.

The role focuses on applying established analytics practices to routine and moderately complex requests, ensuring data accuracy, and helping stakeholders understand and act on data

Key Responsibilities


Analyse data from multiple data sources and partner with subject matter experts to
surface trends and meaningful relationship between variables.
Produce ad hoc reports and visualizations and prepare PPW-ready insights
Engage with internal clients to clarify requests and debrief findings
Deliver scheduled reports and respond to stakeholder questions
Contribute to data audits, cleansing, and follow-up checks to ensure data quality
Collaborate with colleagues to maintain consistent analytics methods


Skills & Experience


Bachelor’s degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Finance, Data Science, or similar
1-3 years of data analytics experience
Background in data analysis, reporting, or analytics (Power BI, SQL,Google Bigquery, Microsoft Excel, etc.)
Experience working with datasets and developing clear, insightful Power BI visualizations.


The Ideal Candidate


Possesses excellent attention to detail, ensuring accuracy and consistency across all reporting and dashboard outputs
Strong communication skills, with ability to communicate effectively to large group.
Effectively manages time and workload, with the ability to work independently.

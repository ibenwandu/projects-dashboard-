About The Role

The Research and Intelligence team supports consulting teams across practices and industries at FTI Consulting in providing the research and analysis needed to deliver engagements and pitches and develop intellectual capital. The research analyst's core responsibilities revolves around handling requests from consultants and supporting seniors in the team in developing internal products and services. The analyst also handles some knowledge management and admin tasks to help the team manage its portfolio of services.

What You'll Do


Support requests from all project and business development teams
Independently handle low complexity of requests with minimal to no guidance from seniors
Support the team in developing internal products and services across industries
Present findings in a clear and structured way
Deliver error-free and exhaustive research outcomes
Develop knowledge on the industries supported to build subject matter expertise path through ongoing learning and development
Deliver timely and consistent admin-based tasks
Master all internal processes in the team and identify opportunities for improvement
Support seniors in handling projects' wrap-up and related knowledge management efforts

## Full job description

Hiring: Intermediate IT Business Analyst

Location: Toronto, Onsite (5 days a week in office)

Duration: April 1, 2026 – March 31, 2027 (possibility to extend)

Hourly Rate: $65/hour - $85/hour (based on experience)

Client: Public Sector - Healthcare




Responsibilities:

The Business Analyst will be responsible for the support and analytical activities related to Provincial Client Registry (PCR), Provincial Provider Registry (PPR), Monitoring and Control Application (MCTA), Provincial Consent Override Interface (PCOI) and Consent Management Technology Asset (CMTA).




Deliverables include, but are not limited to:

Contribute towards the Integrated Program Roadmaps reflecting the timelines and deliverables
Define requirements for the PCR, PPR, MCTA, PCOI and CMTA integrations
Define requirements for enhancement of PCOI interfaces
Define requirements for PCR, PPR, MCTA, PCOI and CMTA support models
Define requirements for transaction reports for data disclosure and operational reports
Document process for leveraging the EHR consent and override methodology within the EHR




Must Haves:

Minimum 4-5 years’ business analyst experience within electronic health-related projects, ideally with experience with clinical software systems
Minimum 2 -3 years’ experience with and knowledge of business analyst concepts
Minimum 2-3 years’ experience with healthcare information systems and data workflows
Excellent communication skills both verbal and written, and strong stakeholder engagement skills
Collaboration, coordinating tasks, and ensuring that everybody works together effectively, this also involves conflict resolution
Demonstrated experience managing projects or programs in matrix organizational environments




Desired Skills:

College/University undergraduate degree in Health, Computer Science, Engineering, Business or related discipline from a recognized institution or equivalent experience
Knowledge of health information data flows
Experience with health system business implementation and deployment projects
Experience in liaising with customers/stakeholders where customer service was a key focus to responding to queries, issues/risk management, etc.
Experience documenting and refining business requirements

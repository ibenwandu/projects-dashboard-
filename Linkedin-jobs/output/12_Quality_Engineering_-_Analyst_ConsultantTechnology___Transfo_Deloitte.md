About The Team

As an Analyst/Consultant in Deloitte’s Technology & Transformation practice specializing in Quality Engineering, you will work closely with cross-functional teams to ensure the design, development, testing and implementation of high-quality, custom-built and AI-driven applications and cloud solutions for our clients. Your role will be pivotal in translating business requirements into robust, testable, and reliable technical solutions, leveraging your expertise in quality assurance, test automation, application modernization, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cloud platforms to drive excellence throughout the software development lifecycle

Enough about us, let’s talk about you

Consultant (Quality Engineering):

A Consultant in Quality Engineering is a hands-on contributor who works as part of a team to implement and execute quality engineering and assurance activities throughout the software development lifecycle, with minimal guidance, to deliver project outcomes.


Years of Experience: 2-3 years of experience in IT, with direct exposure to quality engineering, software testing, or test automation projects.
Key Focus: This role demands a proven ability to contribute to the execution of end-to-end testing projects, including test automation and CI/CD integration. Must have foundational knowledge of test strategies, automation frameworks, and a proactive approach to problem-solving in quality assurance contexts


Analyst (Quality Engineering):

An Analyst in Quality Engineering is a junior professional at the start of their career with foundational knowledge in software testing and quality assurance. They are expected to follow established testing processes and methodologies to support the team in delivering high-quality project outcomes.


Years of Experience: 0-2 years of experience in IT or a related field, with a keen interest in quality engineering, test automation, and software quality practices.
Key Focus: This role requires strong eagerness to learn and a desire to contribute to both technical and process aspects of quality engineering. A foundational understanding of testing concepts, defect management, and a proactive mindset are essential.


For both roles, we are looking for candidates who have the following qualifications:


Foundational understanding of software quality assurance concepts, including manual and automated testing, test case design, defect management, and test strategy development.
Some experience or knowledge of test automation tools (e.g., Selenium, Tricentis Tosca, JUnit) and CI/CD pipelines (e.g., Jenkins, Docker).
Familiarity with testing methodologies (e.g., Agile, Waterfall, Hybrid) and best practices in quality engineering.
Strong ability to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams, identify quality risks, and address them effectively.
Experience in a professional services or consulting environment is preferred.
Familiarity with project management tools and frameworks (e.g., Agile, Scrum).
Foundational understanding of technical concepts such as APIs, web services, object-oriented programming (e.g., Java, Python), and performance testing.
Awareness of emerging technologies in quality engineering, such as Generative AI for testing, is a plus.
Exceptional verbal and written communication skills to effectively translate quality-related concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences.
Self-starter, able to work independently and as part of a cross-functional team.
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Information Technology, or a related field.
Relevant certifications (e.g., ISTQB, Certified Tester, or cloud certifications) are an asset.

## Full job description

Work Model: Hybrid – 4 days onsite

Duration: 8 months




About the role:

We are seeking two (2) Business Analyst V – Internal Audit professionals to support our client’s Deputy Chief Auditor group. This role is a backfill supporting multiple project‑based audits within a collaborative team of 25–30 members. The position offers exposure to diverse capital markets projects, strong team culture, and cross‑training opportunities. Contractors will work with both internal and external stakeholders and will have access to customer data.




Responsibilities

Must‑Have Responsibilities:

Conduct audit work aligned with pre‑established audit plans and professional audit standards
Execute control testing including walkthroughs (leading and documentation), test script development/execution, and issue management
Assist in developing audit documentation such as APM, pRCM, Findings Grid, and Audit Reports
Perform L1 reviews/sign‑offs for audit activities (walkthroughs, test scripts, results, evidence uploads)
Support audit planning processes and complete risk assessments
Maintain ongoing audit communication with stakeholders throughout the audit lifecycle
Present objective audit opinions and recommendations to stakeholders and audit management
Execute follow‑up on findings from internal and regulatory audits
Provide subject matter expertise in risk identification and control evaluation
Participate in daily meetings and collaborate cross‑functionally with ~50% external stakeholder interaction

Experience

5–8 years of relevant internal audit or similar experience
Experience independently executing end‑to‑end audit procedures
Experience working in fast‑paced, regulated environments; capital markets audit exposure preferred
Demonstrated ability to deliver high‑quality outputs under tight deadlines
Strong experience working cross‑functionally and supporting multi‑project environments

Skillsets

Strong audit methodology knowledge
Proficiency in MS Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Access, Outlook)
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
Attention to detail and strong organizational skills
Ability to work independently, follow instructions, escalate appropriately
Ability to work collaboratively in large team environments




Education

Undergraduate degree in Finance, Accounting, or Business
CPA or CIA required
CFA or FRM considered an asset

## Full job description

Role Impact:

The Analyst, Research & Insights, is responsible for providing support for the development and maintenance of our internal insight tools. This position is responsible for supporting the building and servicing of robust self-service tools that provide accessible, actionable insights. This role will primarily focus on CF’s Canadian portfolio of retail and office assets, but could explore other asset classes and geographies as needed. This role collaborates with various internal departments, at all levels of management, including leasing, operations, marketing, development, finance, legal and investments, to create and execute strategic projects.

The Analyst, Research & Insights will use their background in data analysis, visualization, and reporting tools to help in the transformation of data into actionable insights. They will create user-friendly reports and presentations that not only empower stakeholders to make informed decisions but also contribute to growing the organization's analytical capabilities and fostering a culture of data-driven decision-making.

What you will deliver:


Support the end-to-end ESRI Enterprise ecosystem to democratize spatial intelligence, delivering a centralized self-service portal that empowered non-technical stakeholders to independently access and analyze complex datasets.
Perform intermediate spatial and market analysis, including trade area development, competitive mapping, and customer location analytics to provide actionable insights for retail, office, and residential sectors.
Support the team with research analysis and insights that facilitate the delivery of CF’s strategic plan and special projects requested by the executive team and senior management. This could include analyses regarding growth opportunities, operational efficiency and effectiveness, innovation concepts, and business model impacts from industry changes and new trends.
Through the use of tools, support high-level project requirements into an outline of required research and analyses to satisfy the project’s deliverables; establish work plans, monitor progress against goals, and conduct status updates to adjust approaches as required
Support the day-to-day delivery of project/analysis insights, including scope definition, priority setting, research, analysis, insight synthesis, report creation and communication, including updates of CF’s business environment assessment and key business drivers, competitive and industry advances, and other analyses as required.
Collaborate with project team members from across the business to gain input on approaches, analyses, and the feasibility of recommendations, as well as outline pilot approaches and/or rollout plans
Stay current on, new industry trends or disruptors in commercial real estate, retail and office, and technology advances/changes impacting these industries; over time increase knowledge of other asset classes and geographies that CF owns and operates in
Prepare and present memoranda and presentations for senior management, including the executive team
Periodically assist other departments including leasing, operations, marketing, development, finance, legal and investments on special projects or other strategic analyses



What your strengths are:


Solid interpersonal skills and proven track record of influencing, excellent verbal and written communication, naturally curious and confident
Solid critical thinking and problem-solving skills
Strong analytical and quantitative skills; the ability to analyze and interpret large amounts of data and distil important concepts
Thrives in a team environment, inspiring collaboration and thoughtful debate
A motivated self-starter with a strong work ethic and a high level of attention to detail
Strong organizational and prioritization skills
Ability to work independently, multitask and manage priorities in a demanding and continually changing, fast-paced environment, often with multiple or competing deadlines



What you need to succeed:


An undergraduate or post-graduate degree in Geography, Real Estate or Business/Commerce
A minimum of 3 years of work experience in a multi-sector real estate, consulting, retail, private equity, or investment banking firm in a financial analysis, internal consulting, or corporate strategy capacity
Experience with Alteryx, Snowflake, Tableau, ESRI suite and programming languages such as Python/SQL is required
Proficient in spatial analysis and quantitative data visualization
Experience with the Environics suite of products is considered an asset
Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, and PowerPoint), Statistics and use of statistical software such as; SPSS
Highly proficient analytical/quantitative/financial modelling skills in Microsoft Excel, as well as presentation creation skills in PowerPoint; Possession of (or working towards) a CFA/MBA or other professional designation is considered an asset
Real estate industry intelligence knowledge is considered an asset

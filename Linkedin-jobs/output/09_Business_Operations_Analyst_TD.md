Description Du Poste

TD Securities Corporate and Investment Banking (CIB) delivers financial advisory and capital-raising solutions to top-tier corporate and institutional clients. The group operates within a high-performance, client-centric culture and is organized by product, region, and industry specialization.

The Business Management Team within CIB serves as a strategic enabler for senior executives and Relationship Managers by delivering high-impact operational oversight, enterprise program execution, and leadership engagement strategy. This high-visibility team ensures CIB leaders are equipped to focus on their core mandate, serving clients, by overseeing governance, continuity planning, workplace experience, and change management efforts across the platform.

Position Overview

The Business Operations Analyst will lead the ongoing operations of TD Securities’ Toronto CIB office by ensuring seamless workplace experience, facilities coordination, and operational process execution. This role is ideal for a proactive, organized individual who thrives in a dynamic, high-expectation environment and is eager to contribute to both team goals and firm-wide priorities.

The individual will serve as a primary point of contact for CIB Toronto office operations. This role is expected to act with a high level of autonomy, influence cross-functional stakeholders, and continuously enhance operational efficiency across the CIB platform. The typical hours for this role are 8:30am-5:00pm with additional flexibility as needed.

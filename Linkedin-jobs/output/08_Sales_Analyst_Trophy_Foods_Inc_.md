## Full job description

Are you ready to make a real impact and grow your career in an industry that nourishes the nation? Join us at Trophy Foods Inc. in Mississauga, ON, where exciting opportunities await you! Whether you’re looking to launch your career or take it to the next level, we offer the perfect platform to thrive in the dynamic world of food manufacturing.




At Trophy Foods, we don’t just make snacks — we craft quality products that people across the country love. From sourcing the finest ingredients to producing and packaging private label nuts, seeds, dried fruit, mixes, and confections, our team plays a key role in delivering delicious, healthy snacks to households nationwide. With our products featured in grocery stores, clubs, and mass channels, you’ll feel a sense of pride knowing the snacks you helped create are enjoyed coast-to-coast.




Now’s the time to be part of our growth and success! Ready to start or accelerate your journey with us? Come be part of something bigger at Trophy Foods!




We are looking for a Sales Analyst to join our dynamic team at Trophy Foods. This position is for an existing vacancy and offers the opportunity to:

Contribute to our mission of inspiring, creating, and delivering high-quality snacks with exceptional service.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to drive innovation and meet the evolving needs of our customers and consumers.
Ensure the highest standards of food safety, quality, and customer satisfaction are met.
Uphold Trophy’s strong commitment to caring for customers, our community, and the environment.




Overview: Responsible for gathering, analyzing data, and providing actionable, insightful recommendations to optimize sales activities in order to retain, win, and secure business.




Key responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

Compile, distribute and maintain sales reports, sales analysis, and sales information requests for internal use on a weekly, monthly, and quarterly basis.
Gather and analyze data in order to understand problems and address them proactively.
Present actionable information and insights to team members and management.
Participate and share insightful information during Consensus Meetings.
Engage with all customer portal completions for assigned customers including price changes, new listing, and delistings.
Accurate internal customer price changes and notifications.
Timely and accurate updates for all New Product Development (NPD) and Sku Discontinuation (SD) processes.
All promotional tracker updates including Display Ready Pallets (DRP) allocations and promotional volumes are ordered as forecasted.
Work with NAMs in the collection of data to ensure invalid deductions are repaid in a reasonable time frame.
Promote an improvement culture by continuously looking for process enhancements to ensure data integrity and accuracy.
Solve for aging inventory issues in conjunction with NAMs.
Coordinate customer sample orders.
Assist in the coordination of sales events.
Work on ad hoc projects as required.
Manage competing deadlines effectively.




Experience, Qualifications, and Educational Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in business, accounting, marketing, or another related field.
2 – 3 years of related experience in CPG and manufacturing environment.
Demonstrated sales database management/data integrity capability.
Demonstrated experience supporting all sales functions.
Experience tracking and reporting on sales related metrics.
Experience working with large amounts of data and summarizing information into usable format for decision-making.
Strong customer portal experience (Retail Link, Circana, etc.).
High level of proficiency in MS Office, Outlook, Excel (formulas – Vlookups, sums, pivot tables) and PowerPoint.
Experience with Power BI (i.e. Building Dashboard) an asset.
Prior experience with an ERP environment, with knowledge of JD Edwards and Hubble an asset.
Problem solver with a good attitude toward teamwork.
Confident and competent managing tasks that involve cross functional teams, and time sensitive processes.
Excellent time management with demonstrated ability to multitask and prioritize.
Bilingual (French & English) an asset.
Experience with Power BI (i.e. building Dashboard) an asset.

## Full job description

The Logistics Analyst is responsible for monitoring and ensuring all inland and overseas shipments for MCPE units arrive on time and updated into warehouses in an effective timely manner. Troubleshoot transport delays and resolve issues quickly to keep containers moving throughout the supply chain. The incumbent is also responsible for Inventory and Freight Reconciliations.

Responsibilities


Responsible for Vessel Log / ETA file, enter shipment information including model, colour, quantity, factory, destination, carrier, container number, ETD and ETA. Update as required when delivery changes occur and email to distribution list on a weekly basis
Monitor lead time performance by factory and warehouse.
Coordinate Vessel Processing, trigger flow of goods through Finance system and send transmission to OPS and K&N which allows receiving of units into stock
Investigate and resolve any errors or issues by engaging with IT, S&PP and/or Factory
Responsible for GE Ship Orders including manual order entry is required for models ordered from factories that do not transmit manifest data to CH
Provide updated ETA information and reason for delay (if applicable)
Provide pipeline report to distribution list on key target dates within the month.
Assist with global bid nominations by providing feedback to Honda Motor.
Responsible for monthly book to book inventory reconciliation.
Resolve discrepancies with assistance from OPS, K&N, SSG, and/or other departments. Make system corrections when necessary or advise warehouse on appropriate action to be taken.
Ensure FIFO (first in first out) method is being properly employed by warehouse.
Responsible for Annual Physical Count – conducted at an agreed upon date with Sales, Finance and warehouses. The warehouses shut down for 1 day to physically count all MCPE units within the warehouse, typically a 4-month project. Results are provided and reviewed by Finance prior to audit.
Prepare Risk Management Report update, which is sent to Corporate Governance team.
Assist with customer service/support, resolve issues and assist the Sales Group
Manually update stock as requested, stock transfer, flagging and unflagging of units
Expedite container arrival as requested to support sales targets
Assist OPSCS and K&N with warehouse issues and investigate possible solutions to improve efficiency.
Monitor warehouse key performance indicators (KPI).
Monitor accuracy and completion of invoices.

What will your typical day look like?


Process employee data transactions in SAP SuccessFactors and downstream HR Systems, run reports and monitor data quality overall
Perform ongoing validation, reconciliation, and audit of employee data
Support UAT testing for HRIS and reporting enhancement projects and quarterly system upgrades
Maintain and create training materials, SOP’s and user guides
Deliver excellent service, in line with process requirements, ensuring a high level of accuracy, quality and turnaround of work within agreed timelines.
Support pre-hire and post-hire employee data collection from 90+ countries required by various People COE’s
Work with People Data Management Platform Product Owner and People Data Quality Leader to support data uploads, data maintenance process, conduct data audit and reconciliation to help achieve business objectives
Translate user needs into specific data requirements
Identify relevant data sources within People Data Management Platform
Test data products and ensure they meet user requirements
Document data products so others can locate and use when needed
Understand security requirements and implications for end user

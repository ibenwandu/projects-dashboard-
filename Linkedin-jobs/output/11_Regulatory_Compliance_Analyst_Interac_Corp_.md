Who You Will Work With:

This is a vacant role reporting into the Leader, ERM Regulatory Oversight and an opportunity to join a growing team within a great organization that supports growth and professional development.

What You Will Do:

The ERM Regulatory Oversight Analyst is a key contributor to Interac’s regulatory compliance and risk management objectives. This role is responsible for executing day-to-day regulatory oversight activities, ensuring timely delivery of commitments, and maintaining accurate documentation. Core responsibilities include supporting audits and reviews, tracking and reporting on regulatory deliverables, and coordinating with business units to drive compliance across the organization. The Specialist plays a critical role in aligning Interac’s practices with regulatory requirements under the Retail Payment Activities Act (RPAA), Prominent Payment System (PPS), and related frameworks such as OSFI Guideline E-21 and PCI DSS.


Execute regulatory oversight activities in alignment with Interac’s PPS and RPAA Regulatory Oversight Framework.
Maintain and update regulatory documentation to ensure accuracy, completeness, and audit readiness.
Support audits, Core Assurance Reviews and regulatory examinations by coordinating oversight and documentation activities across the enterprise.
Maintain and manage a calendar of regulatory activities and reporting obligations, ensuring timely tracking, monitoring and reporting of deliverables.
Facilitate RPAA onboarding and regulatory reporting in collaboration with business units.
Ensure alignment between obligations, controls and reporting requirements.
Support integration of regulatory requirements into change management and product development/IT development processes.
Integrate regulatory oversight requirements into change management and product development processes.
Contribute to promoting a sound risk culture across the enterprise.

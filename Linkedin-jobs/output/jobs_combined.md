Introduction To Role

Are you ready to turn complex spend data into decisions that elevate supplier performance and value across the Americas? Do you thrive on connecting analytics with business impact to help resources reach the programs that matter most for patients and the business?

In this role on our Americas Procurement Category team, you will support categories based in the region including Media, Advertising, Medical Communications, Market Access, Patient Programs, and Contract Sales Force. Your insight and judgement will help channel investment effectively, reduce risk, and accelerate outcomes that power our enterprise and, ultimately, patient impact.

Reporting to the Senior Director Category Management Americas, you will partner with Directors and Associate Directors to manage defined spend categories or markets at country, regional or global scope. You will evaluate suppliers, analyze data, and drive value-focused decisions that optimize total cost of ownership, strengthen supplier performance, and ensure continuity and compliance.

Recognizing the importance of individualized flexibility, our ways of working allow employees to balance personal and work commitments while ensuring we continue to create a strong culture of collaboration and teamwork by engaging face-to-face in our offices 3 days a week in the office. Our head office is purposely designed with collaboration in mind, providing space where teams can come together to strategize, brainstorm and connect on key projects. 

Accountabilities


Category Partnership: Work with Directors/Associate Directors to manage a spend category or part of a market across a country, region, or the whole enterprise, translating category strategy into sourcing decisions that deliver measurable value.
Supplier Evaluation: Evaluate suppliers objectively and consistently to inform selection and negotiation, balancing capability, performance, value, and risk.
Data and Insight Generation: Analyze spend, market, and performance data to produce actionable insights that improve procurement outcomes and inform leadership decisions.
Total Cost Optimization: Identify and recommend opportunities that reduce total cost of ownership while safeguarding quality, service, and supply resilience.
Supplier Performance Management: Track performance against commitments; highlight gaps and collaborate on corrective actions that strengthen delivery and outcomes.
Risk Mitigation: Anticipate and assess supplier and market risks; support contingency planning and proactive interventions to protect business continuity.
Stakeholder Engagement: Partner with cross-functional teams across categories such as Media, Advertising, Medical Communications, Market Access, Patient Programs, and Contract Sales Force to align requirements, trade-offs, and timelines.
Progression and Impact: Deliver immediate analytical support and sourcing execution, then expand influence to shape category strategies and enterprise standards.

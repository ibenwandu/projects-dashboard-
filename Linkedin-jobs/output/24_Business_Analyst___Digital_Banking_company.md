Business Analyst – IT (Advanced) – Digital Banking

Location: Hybrid – 88 Queens Quay W, Toronto

Duration: March 3, 2026 – March 2, 2027

Rate: $63–$73/hr INC

We are seeking a Senior Business Analyst with strong experience in digital technologies and online/mobile banking platforms to support medium to large-scale digital transformation initiatives within an enterprise financial services environment. This role will focus on delivering end-to-end requirements for customer-facing digital banking solutions, including web and mobile applications.

Key Responsibilities:

Lead requirements planning, elicitation, analysis, documentation, and support for online and mobile banking initiatives
Facilitate large stakeholder workshops (15–20+ participants) across Digital, Product, Technology, and UX teams
Translate business and customer experience requirements into detailed user stories and technical specifications
Develop current and future state process models, scope models, and data mappings
Work closely with UI/UX teams to design and refine digital application interfaces
Analyze API integrations, JSON/XML payloads, and system data flows
Lead Agile ceremonies alongside Product Owners in a fast-paced digital delivery environment
Support build, testing, and implementation phases ensuring full requirements traceability
Mentor junior BAs and provide consulting support on digital initiatives

Must-Have Skills:

8–10+ years of BA/BSA experience in Agile project environments
Proven experience supporting online and/or mobile banking projects
Strong expertise in digital requirements documentation (user stories, story mapping, NFRs, acceptance criteria)
Experience with APIs, system integrations, and data mapping
Proficiency with JIRA, Confluence, Visio, Mural, Figma, and related tools
Strong stakeholder facilitation skills in large digital programs

Nice to Have:

Experience in enterprise digital transformation programs
Financial services industry experience
BA certification/designation

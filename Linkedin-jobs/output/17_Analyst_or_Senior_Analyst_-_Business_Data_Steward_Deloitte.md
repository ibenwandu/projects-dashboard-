The newly formed Data & Insights Centre of Excellence is looking to hire a Business Data Steward to join a strong and dynamic Data Governance Team to help in the management and governance of Contact and Account Data in our brand new local and global systems.

What will your typical day look like?

As a Business Data Steward with the D&I COE you will be responsible for verifying and/or updating data in and across our new local and global data management systems. As part of your role, you will address inquiries/requests submitted by practitioners and leaders and work with them to have their data updated in the system. When necessary, you will be asked to perform research of external sources to validate information and confirm accuracy.

In addition to the day-to-day maintenance and creation of data, there will be lots of opportunity to help shape Data Governance in the form of building out new business processes, implementing controls, and managing small sized initiatives/projects for data cleanup, creation, or net new functionality.

About The Team

The BDS team is a group of onshore and offshore Data Stewards who work diligently to uphold the integrity of data in our source systems. We work in tandem with global data management teams to ensure alignment of data with other member firms. We are a powerful team that has a customer service-oriented approach, we work hard but also make time for fun.

Enough about us, let’s talk about you

You are someone with these required skills:


Working knowledge of a CRM for managing customer and client data. Salesforce experience would be an asset.
Bi-lingual in French and English both written and spoken. (Due to the nature of the role having interactions with National & Global clients, English is required for this position)
Experience in conducting secondary research.
Understanding of data governance principles and best practices.
Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel, including complex formulas and data analysis tools.
Foundational knowledge of SQL for querying and managing relational databases.
Awareness of data quality frameworks and techniques to ensure accurate and reliable information.


It would be great for you to have some of these nice-to-haves as well:


Familiarity with CASL (Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation) and its application in business communications.
Experience creating interactive dashboards and reports using Tableau or PowerBI.
Proficiency in developing process flows and diagrams using Microsoft Visio.
Demonstrated ability to support project coordination activities across cross-functional teams.
Exposure to Generative AI (Gen AI) technologies and their business applications.

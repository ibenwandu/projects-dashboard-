## Full job description

Purpose

Support the interface to internal teams while coordinating and developing the applications to efficiently achieve sales and service targets that support the goal of achieving on time delivery and strategic business priorities.

Key Responsibilities


Conducts end to end review of Customer Applications specific processes to optimize resources and time making sure they process used is the most efficient way of working
Supports the daily workflow direction to Application Representatives (ApS) to ensure that Department needs are met in an accurate, timely and professional manner within the guidelines of established company policies and procedures.
Facilitates the IT generated changes such as system upgrades or fold in projects. These duties may include the execution of test scripts related to key processes (e.g. allocation), and supporting the testing completion of other members of the CS Department.
Support cross functional relationships to discuss and seek resolution to problem solve issues discovered during the analysis of various application processes
Actively support in specialized training, either in a one-on-one format or in small classes setting against our application tool kit.
Represent Order Management team as subject matter expert on cross-functional projects
Support ad hoc execution of transactions in mass as needed and at the request of senior leadership
Monitor internal request process to ensure timely and accurate responses to business needs
Identify training opportunities, develop training documentation, and lead training sessions
Provide team direction, prioritize team workload, and manage project timelines and deliverables
Manage the day-to-day details of projects and coordinate with relevant stakeholders

Knowledge Skills And Abilities


SAP expertise in Sales & Distribution, Process Automation.
Ability to collaborate, consult, and communicate with business partners and stakeholders
MS Office (Excel, Outlook, Word, Powerpoint, Teams, Sharepoint) and Smartsheets
Result orientated and pro-active, good decision and problem-solving skills
Works independently and proactively manages work
Demonstrates a sense of urgency
Reviews data while identifying trends and initiating improvement actions
Challenge existing processes and develop unique solutions to problems
Strong organizational and planning skills


Requisite Education And Experience / Minimum Qualifications


Bachelor's degree or College Diploma in Business Administration, Information Systems, Logistics, Supply Chain, or relevant field.
A minimum of 3 years' experience in a similar role.

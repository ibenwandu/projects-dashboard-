## Full job description

Key Responsibilities

Assess end-to-end payment processes including High-Value Payments (HVP), Low-Value Payments (LVP), FX processing, liquidity management, and nostro/vostro accounting to identify improvement opportunities.
Develop current-state and future-state process maps using standard process-mapping tools.
Apply Lean / Lean Six Sigma methodologies to eliminate waste, reduce operational errors, and streamline workflows.
Collaborate with business, operations, and technology teams to implement process improvements and ensure successful adoption.
Conduct root-cause analysis and structured problem-solving for operational issues within payment systems.
Ensure process improvements align with banking risk, compliance, and operational control frameworks.
Provide data-driven recommendations to improve operational efficiency, reduce cycle times, and enhance service stability.




Required Payments Knowledge

Strong understanding of High-Value and Low-Value payment systems
Knowledge of FX products and end-to-end FX payment processing
Familiarity with nostro/vostro accounting and reconciliation processes
Understanding of intraday and end-of-day liquidity management
Knowledge of operational risk and control frameworks in payments environments
Working knowledge of SWIFT messaging and ISO 20022 standards for payments and cash management




Qualifications

5+ years of experience in payments operations, process improvement, or banking operations roles
Lean or Lean Six Sigma certification (Green Belt or higher)
Strong process mapping, documentation, and workflow analysis skills
Experience collaborating across business, operations, and technology teams
Strong analytical thinking, structured problem-solving, and continuous improvement mindset

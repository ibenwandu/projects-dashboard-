About the Role

A leading Bank is seeking a Senior Financial Analyst to join its Enterprise Real Estate (ERE) Finance team. This team supports a national portfolio of office and branch locations, providing financial oversight, reporting, forecasting, lease analysis, and project accounting support.

This is a 9-month contract opportunity with strong potential for extension or conversion to full-time.




Key Responsibilities

Develop financial models and business cases related to real estate initiatives
Support RFP processes and contract renewals
Conduct financial impact assessments and projection modeling
Provide ad-hoc financial analysis to support procurement and real estate decisions
Collaborate with internal stakeholders to support budgeting, forecasting, and reporting
Ensure compliance with financial and accounting policies




Qualifications

5–7 years of analytical experience, including 3+ years in financial analysis and modeling (consulting experience acceptable)
Exposure to real estate accounting or financial support within a real estate function
Strong Excel and PowerPoint skills
Experience supporting RFP projects and contract negotiations
Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills
University degree (Business preferred)

## Full job description

Reporting to the Director, Security Operations, the successful candidate will manage all hardware and software assets, ensuring the efficient use of resources and cost-effectiveness. This role will be an important part of Fasken’s overall strategy and will establish an asset management process (ITAM) to provide up-to-date information to reduce risks and optimize costs.

Hours of work are from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. ET. Occasional pre-authorized overtime may be required to meet client needs. This is a hybrid role.

Primary Responsibilities


Asset Management
Maintain an accurate inventory of all hardware and software assets
Track and manage the lifecycle of assets, including acquisition, deployment, maintenance, and disposal
Ensure compliance with software licensing agreements and avoid over-licensing or under-licensing
Contract and Agreement Management
Oversee contract renewals and manage vendor relationships to ensure the firm is receiving maximum value
Monitor and analyze contract performance to identify cost-saving opportunities
Provide regular reports to application & system owners on upcoming license and maintenance renewals
Ensure timely renewals and adherence to contract terms
Vendor Management
Negotiate with vendors and suppliers to secure favorable terms and pricing
Develop and maintain strong relationships with key vendors and suppliers
Evaluate and select vendors based on service, quality, cost, and reliability
Coordinate with internal governance teams on vendor risk management
Invoice & Cost Management
Ensure proper coding and timely payment of IT invoices
Identify and implement strategies to reduce costs and improve efficiency in asset utilization
Applying FinOps best practices to monitor and analyze cloud environments and create recommendations based on budget, best practices, timelines, and goals
Monitor, control, and assess budget alignment for expenditures related to hardware and software assets
Provide regular reports on asset performance, usage, and cost-saving measures
Policy and Procedure Development
Develop and enforce policies and procedures for asset management
Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements related to IT assets
Train and educate staff on asset management best practices

Required Knowledge And Experience


Bachelor’s degree in information technology, Business Administration, or a related field
Minimum 3 years' experience in asset management, preferably within a law firm or corporate environment
Strong negotiation and vendor management skills
Excellent analytical and problem-solving abilities
Proficient in asset management software and tools
Strong communication and interpersonal skills
Ability to work independently and as part of a team

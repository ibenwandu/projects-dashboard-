## Full job description

Job Description

Metrolinx is connecting communities across the Greater Golden Horseshoe. Metrolinx operates GO Transit and UP Express, as well as the PRESTO fare payment system. We are also building new and improved rapid transit, including GO Expansion, Light Rail Transit routes, and major expansions to Toronto’s subway system, to get people where they need to go, better, faster and easier. Metrolinx is an agency of the Government of Ontario.

At Metrolinx, equity, diversity and inclusion are essential to living our values of serving with passion, thinking forward and playing as a team.

Reporting to Manager, Operational Technology, we are looking to hire a Business Analyst – Technology & Data, who will play a key role in ensuring that technology initiatives are delivered efficiently, effectively, and in alignment with business needs. Your work will bridge business objectives with technical solutions and support the successful implementation of technology projects across the operation.

What will I be doing?


Translate business goals, processes, and pain points into clear system requirements, scope definitions, workflows, SOPs, and technical specifications while identifying opportunities to automate or improve workflows.
Lead and support testing by developing test plans and scenarios, guiding users through execution, monitoring results, and recommending solutions for identified issues.
Convert business requirements into technical requirements and provide ongoing support and guidance during technology projects, including planning, analysis, tracking, transition activities, and issue resolution.
Facilitate communication between business teams, IT, and vendors; support business cases, risk identification, prioritization, change management activities, training material development, and creation of project status reports or dashboards.
Collaborate with stakeholders to gather and interpret qualitative and quantitative data, ensuring decisions align with business needs and customer-focused outcomes.
Promote operational excellence by monitoring documentation standards, identifying process or technology optimization opportunities, supporting training and readiness for new systems, and providing flexibility for extended hours during major technology changes.



What Skills and Qualifications Do I Need?


Post‑secondary education in a related field (e.g., Computer Science, Information Systems, Engineering, Business Process Improvement) or an equivalent combination of training and experience, with proven Business Analyst experience—ideally in transportation, technology, or the public sector.
Experience supporting technology projects, system implementations, process automation, and data‑driven decision-making, along with strong knowledge of technology concepts, system development practices, and business process design.
Proficiency with analytical and quantitative tools (data modelling, workflow mapping, process analysis) and with MS Office applications such as Outlook, Word, Excel, Visio, PowerPoint, and Project.
Experience participating in testing cycles, including planning, execution, and reporting.
Strong analytical, critical‑thinking, problem‑solving, communication, and stakeholder‑management skills, with the ability to turn complex business needs into clear requirements.
Highly organized, collaborative team player with the ability to manage multiple priorities in fast‑paced environments; PMP, CBAP, or similar certifications are considered assets.

## Full job description

CONTRACT: 6 months, chance for extension

START DATE: ASAP

SETUP: Remote **MUST RESIDE IN ONTARIO**




RESPONSIBILITIES:

Revenue Accounting & ASC 606 Architecture (40%)

Audit the end-to-end Revenue Recognition process. Transition the organization from manual "Excel patches" to automated, system-driven revenue waterfalls.
Partner with the Systems Manager to validate the Snowflake Customer Data Cube. Ensure the bridge between usage data and recognized revenue is a "Single Source of Truth" for Board-level NRR and Retention reporting.
Formalize the revenue accounting treatment for new product bundles or contract structures, ensuring immediate compliance with ASC 606.

2. Modernized O2C Interlock (Billing Ops Partnership) (30%)

Work alongside the Billing Operations Manager to design and deploy the new "Fresh AR" workflows.
Act as the strategic lead for moving AR responsibilities from Customer Success into Finance.
Establish the "Zero-Defect" standard for all new FY26 invoices, ensuring Billing Ops has the tools to maintain a <30 day aging for all new business.

3. Automation & "Digital Factory" Build (30%)

Design and launch the automated calculation engine for FY26 commissions and partner revenue-share accruals, removing the manual burden from the Billing team.
Act as the functional lead for optimizing the data flow between Salesforce, Chargebee, and Sage. Eliminate the need for "manual CSV uploads" for new business.




REQUIREMENTS:

Accounting designated
Advanced level Excel skills, including Power Query, VBA and macros
SaaS industry experience
ASC 606 or IFRS 15 Expertise

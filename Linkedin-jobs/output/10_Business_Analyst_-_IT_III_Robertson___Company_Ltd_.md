## Full job description

Our client is a top financial institution with significant North American holdings. They have operations across most major verticals, including institutional & corporate, wealth management, private client, commercial banking, treasury, and retail banking. 
 
Introduction: Robertson is seeking a skilled Business Analyst - IT III to join our clientin support of an existingvacancy.

Contract Period: 10 months with potential to extend
Pay Rate: Starting from $64 per hour
Location: Toronto, ON
Location Type: Hybrid
Business Hours: Monday-Friday;Core Business Hours

Job Responsibilities:
Elicit, document, and prioritize data governance requirements (policies, standards, controls, metrics)
Define DQ rules, thresholds, and KPIs; coordinate issue triage, root-cause analysis, and remediation
Support cataloging, business glossary, and lineage capture; ensure ownership and stewardship are assigned
Translate regulatory and internal policy needs into actionable controls and procedures
Help design RACI, workflows, and approval gates for data ingestion, curation, and access
Facilitate forums with data stewards, product owners, and engineers; track decisions and actions
Map current/future-state processes; identify automation opportunities and standardize best practices
Maintain governance scorecards; produce insights on data health, risk, and adoption
Collaborate with data governance engineers to ensure the runbooks, design documents
Experience & Qualification Requirement:
Bachelor’s degree
5-7 Years of Business Analyst Experience
Experience in data governance, data management, or business analysis
Familiarity with data catalogs (e.g., Informatica CDGC, Microsoft Purview), lineage tools (e.g., Databrick Unity Catalog), DQ platforms (Informatica CDQ), and metadata standards
Strong analytical, communication, and documentation skills; adept at translating business needs into technical requirements
Data governance expertise: Policies, standards, controls, stewardship, operating models (RACI)
Business analysis: Requirements elicitation, prioritization, process mapping (current/future state)
Tools proficiency: Data catalog/lineage/DQ platforms (e.g., Informatica IDMC, Microsoft Purview, Databricks Unity Catalog); familiarity with reporting/BI tools, proficiency with Microsoft suite (e.g., PowerPoint, Excel)
Stakeholder management: Facilitation, consensus-building, decision tracking across business/tech/risk
Communication and documentation: Craft clear, concise, audience-tailored narratives. Produce structured reports, executive summaries with crisp visuals, consistent definitions, and traceable metrics. Maintain rigorous documentation (decision logs, RACI, policy mappings) with version control, accessibility, and audit readiness
Process improvement and automation: Workflow design, standardization, CI/CD awareness
Analytical thinking: Insight generation, KPI interpretation, anomaly identification
Understanding of basic programming language (e.g., SQL & Python) is an asset

## Full job description

Requisition ID: 253694

Join a purpose driven winning team, committed to results, in an inclusive and high-performing culture.

Business Banking is seeking a Business Systems Analyst (BSA) to partner with Business, Product, and Engineering teams to deliver technical solutions aligned to Business Banking needs and requirements for key modernization initiatives.

This role helps teams ship faster and safer by translating business intent into buildable requirements, clarifying dependencies and trade-offs early, and producing decision-ready artifacts that reduce ambiguity and rework.

The work spans a modern application suite that replaces legacy capabilities with API-first, cloud-native, loosely coupled services and experiences, integrating internal and third-party platforms.

Reporting to the Senior Manager Development, Business Banking Domain, you will partner with Business, engineering, product, security, data, infrastructure, and other application teams to deliver production-ready solutions aligned to enterprise standards for security, resiliency, scalability, availability, and regulatory compliance.

You are expected to contribute directly through discovery, documentation, solutioning, mapping, and operational support to ensure solutions meet Business Banking requirements and expectations.

Is this role right for you? In this role, you will:


Participate in requirements gathering and design sessions with multiple stakeholders to extract requirements and create user stories, mapping diagrams and process flows.
Conduct ongoing governance to ensure the accuracy of platform playbooks, PII, and other relevant information.
Review and understand operational objectives by studying business functions; gathering information; evaluating output requirements.
Review new incoming requirements and intakes for technology assessment, ensuring documentation quality, and timely responses.
Contribute to application risk reviews and reporting (e.g., control gaps, change impacts, mitigation options)
Participate in discovery and planning sessions
Comfortable working in a self-guided manner with the ability to prioritize tasks and manage your own time efficiently, without direct input or oversight.
Review new incoming requirements and intakes for technology assessment, ensuring consistent triage, documentation quality, and timely responses.
Support production health by partnering with engineering teams on ticket triage, root-cause analysis support, and prioritization of operational fixes.
Prepare and execute health checks and operational readiness activities during deployments and key releases; document outcomes and follow-ups.
Track and support remediation of security vulnerabilities by coordinating evidence, requirements impacts, and documentation updates.
Maintain and improve platform playbooks, application support materials, and governance artifacts to keep them accurate and easy to use.
Contribute to platform and operational improvements by analyzing current practices, identifying waste/rework, and proposing pragmatic enhancements.
Apply an innovation mindset to continuously improve how requirements are captured, validated, and maintained—reducing ambiguity, cycle time, and rework.
Use AI assistants responsibly to accelerate analysis and documentation (e.g., drafting user stories/acceptance criteria, summarizing workshops, creating traceability matrices, generating test scenario ideas), while validating outputs for correctness and compliance.
Help establish lightweight standards and reusable templates for stories, mappings, and diagrams; promote consistent quality across squads.
Partner with the team to identify opportunities to automate recurring analysis and documentation tasks (within policy and data-handling constraints).

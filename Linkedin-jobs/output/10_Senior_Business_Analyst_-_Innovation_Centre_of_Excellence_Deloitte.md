What will your typical day look like?

As a Senior Business Analyst (Consultant or Senior Consultant level) in the Strategy, Risk & Transactions (SR&T) Innovation Centre of Excellence, you will be part of an incubation team responsible for transforming service delivery across our Strategy, Risk, and M&A & Transactions advisory portfolios. Recent initiatives have focused on leveraging GenAI and other emerging technologies to enhance practitioner efficiency, effectiveness, and quality.

You will play a key role in supporting innovation initiatives being driven across the business by:


Designing conversational AI solutions to automate business workflows and improve user experience amongst our SR&T Practitioners
Building wireframes and prototypes to visualize solution concepts and user journeys, and contributing to high-level solution design decks and presentations
Developing user stories and detailed business requirements in collaboration with stakeholders and technical teams, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure requirements are understood and delivered
Defining scope boundaries for solution features and releases, and tracking feature enhancements and bug fixes
Leading and documenting testing activities, including writing test cases, tracking bugs, and validating enhancements
Creating documentation for asset approvals, including architecture design, business continuity, and disaster recovery plans


About The Team

The SR&T Innovation COE team empowers the Strategy, Risk & Transactions business to achieve sustainable profitable and market leading growth by harnessing the potential of emerging technologies (including GenAI), design-driven innovation approaches, and multidisciplinary talent. By ideating, incubating, and accelerating innovative solutions, we help teams transform, address clients’ most pressing challenges, and deliver differentiated value – ensuring SR&T remains agile and competitive in an ever-evolving market.

Enough about us, let’s talk about you

We are seeking future-focused, collaborative and action-oriented candidates, at the Consultant or Senior Consultant level, who demonstrate:


Strong experience in business analysis, systems analysis, and project coordination
UX design experience building process flow diagrams, wireframes and prototypes leveraging software tools like Figma and Visio
Ability to write clear, actionable user stories and business requirements
Ability to harness AI technologies like M365 Copilot Chat, Copilot Studio, and Gemini to create intelligent workflows and agentic solutions
Knowledge of asset approval documentation, including architecture design, security controls, business continuity, and disaster recovery plans for software solutions
Familiarity with Azure cloud architecture and LLM/GenAI concepts
Experience with testing, bug tracking, and feature management in JIRA
Strong analytical and innovative mindset with a passion for emerging technologies
Effective communication and stakeholder management skills
Management consulting experience and/or CPA, CBV, or Project Management certifications are considered strong assets

### 7. Psychology of Early Exits

#### Prospect Theory & Loss Aversion
- [Psychology of Taking Profits Too Early](https://fxtribune.com/the-psychology-of-taking-profits-too-early-unlock-trading-success/)
  - Key finding: Losses feel 1.5-2.5x more painful than equivalent gains
  - Result: Traders take profits early and hold losses

- [Can You Hold On to Your Winners?](https://www.babypips.com/trading/psychology-cut-losses-hold-winners-2025-12-18)
  - Common pattern: Win 15 out of 25 trades, but close 15 early
  - Closing early: 3-week gain 2.3% vs 4.3% potential = 44% lost profit

- [Why You Exit Trades Too Early](https://www.learntotradethemarket.com/forex-articles/why-you-exit-trades-too-early-how-to-stop-doing-it/)
  - Root cause: Fear of losing profit (loss aversion)
  - Solution: Pre-define exit plan, use trailing stops, automate

#### Overcoming Early Exit Bias
- [Forex Trading Psychology: The Playbook](https://newyorkcityservers.com/blog/forex-trading-psychology-discipline-guide)
  - Discipline tools: Pre-planned exits, automated orders
  - Removal of discretion: No manual closes except emergency

- [Why Forex Traders Cut Profits](https://www.babypips.com/trading/locking-in-forex-profits-20191007)
  - Key technique: Move stop to breakeven, let rest run

## Key Findings Summary

### For Trade-Alerts System

1. **ATR-based TP is superior to fixed distance**
   - Adapts to volatility
   - Implementation: TP = Entry ± (ATR × 2.0)

2. **Half-and-run strategy solves "close too early" problem**
   - Close 50% at 1.5x risk (locks profit)
   - Trail 50% to 2.5-3.0x risk (captures moves)
   - Expected: 1.5-2.0x average return per winner

3. **EMA exits (21-period) work better than fixed time limits**
   - Exits when trend reverses
   - Use for runners (second 50%)

4. **Psychology is the main problem, not signals**
   - Your entries are good (trades move in right direction)
   - Exits are emotional (early closes for fear of loss)
   - Solution: Automate everything, remove manual intervention

5. **Win rate on first TP should be 80%+**
   - If <80%: Entry signals need work
   - If >80%: Exit strategy is working

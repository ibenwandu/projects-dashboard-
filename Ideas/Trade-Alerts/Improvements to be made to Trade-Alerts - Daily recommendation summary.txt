Improvements to be made to Trade-Alerts - Daily summary

After the first recommendation for the day in UTC time, every subsequest recommendation will come with on update for previous recommendations for that day. The advice will be part of the final recommendation from Gemini. It should be in a tabular format with the fields: Pair(name of the recommended pair), Trade (Sell or Buy), Timeframe (Intraday or Swing), Updated Recommendation (Trade is still valid or Close Trade). This table is carried forward for other subsequent Trade-Alerts, increasing as more trades are recommended for that day. The table resets at midnight everyday (UTC), removes all the non-valid trades (Close Trade) from the previous day but carries forward any viable trade from previous day (Trade still valid).

## Full job description

Job Summary

We are seeking an experienced Data Analyst with 5+ years of experience to collect, analyze, and interpret complex datasets to support business decision-making. The ideal candidate has strong analytical skills, expertise in SQL and Python, and experience building dashboards and reports.

Key Responsibilities

Analyze large datasets to identify trends and insights
Develop and maintain dashboards and reports using BI tools
Write complex SQL queries to extract and manipulate data
Work closely with stakeholders to understand business requirements
Perform data validation and ensure data quality
Present findings and recommendations to leadership
Support data-driven decision-making across departments

Required Qualifications

5+ years of experience as a Data Analyst or similar role
Strong proficiency in SQL
Experience with Python or R for data analysis
Experience with BI tools (Tableau, Power BI, or similar)
Knowledge of data modeling and database concepts
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
Excellent communication and presentation skills

Preferred Skills

Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
Knowledge of ETL processes
Experience working with large datasets
Understanding of data warehousing concepts

## Full job description

Job Title: Senior Analyst, Capital Reporting & Forecasting
Location: North York, ON
Schedule: Hybrid (On-site Monday, Tuesday, Thursday)

Existing Vacancy: Yes

Our client is seeking a Senior Analyst, Capital Reporting & Forecasting to support the consolidation, analysis, and forecasting of capital financials while partnering with key stakeholders across Finance and Asset Management.

What's the Job?

Consolidate capital financials, including monthly and quarterly reporting of budgets, forecasts, and actuals.
Support development of in‑service capital forecasts on a quarterly or ad‑hoc basis.
Provide active financial support and counsel to Asset Management and the Business.
Assist Capital FP&A leadership in advising Finance, Asset Management Governance, and other functional teams.
Consolidate monthly results and perform variance analysis against budget and forecast.
Consolidate forecast inputs from Asset Management and calculate overhead allocations.
Support the annual budgeting cycle, including variance analysis and preparation of financial schedules.
Deliver timely financial support, data, and analysis to internal stakeholders.
Build effective relationships with Asset Management teams to understand and validate forecasting and reporting processes.
Contribute to periodic forecasts, annual budgets, and long‑range planning.
Prepare ad hoc and off‑cycle reports to support management decision-making.
Ensure financial reporting aligns with regulatory frameworks.
Support SOX controls and audit activities.
Contribute to identifying, assessing, mitigating, and managing financial risks.
Assist in communicating policy updates across internal teams.
Perform additional related duties as needed.

What's Needed?

University degree in Business required.
Professional designation (CMA, CA, CPA, CGA, or MBA) preferred.
5+ years of post‑designation experience in business performance analytics or similar roles.
Working knowledge of SAP, Oracle, and Hyperion preferred.
Strong business acumen and understanding of financial management and regulatory reporting.
Proven team collaborator with strong communication, interpersonal, and influencing skills.
Ability to manage multiple priorities and meet tight deadlines.
Experience improving processes and streamlining workflows.
Experience in a regulated environment preferred.
Commitment to the values of Integrity, Safety, and Respect.

What's in it for me?

Opportunity to play a key role in capital reporting, forecasting, and financial planning.
Hybrid work environment offering balance and in‑person collaboration.
Exposure to multiple business units and the opportunity to influence financial decisions.
A collaborative environment focused on accuracy, continuous improvement, and professional growth.
Meaningful involvement in budgeting, SOX compliance, and long‑range forecasting.

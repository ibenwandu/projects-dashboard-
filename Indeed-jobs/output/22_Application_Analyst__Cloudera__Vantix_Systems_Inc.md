Description

Our Client is seeking an Intermediate Application Analyst ( Cloudera ) to provide technical support, troubleshooting, and system maintenance for an Enterprise Database Archival Service on the Cloudera Data Platform. The successful candidate will collaborate closely with end-users, IT teams, and stakeholders to ensure the smooth functioning of the platform. The ideal candidate will possess strong analytical skills, excellent communication abilities, and a passion for delivering exceptional customer service.

The Application Analyst will provide analysis and recommendations on process improvements and to hosting infrastructure, and include details of risks, dependencies, business impacts, interdependencies, and implications pertaining to the methodology to be applied on projects. The role serves as a bridge between users and technical teams, ensuring that applications effectively meet business objectives. A proven ability to function on an individual basis and in a team environment is essential.

While this role is remote, all work must be done from within Canada, due to network and data security issues. Must be Canadian Citizen to apply.

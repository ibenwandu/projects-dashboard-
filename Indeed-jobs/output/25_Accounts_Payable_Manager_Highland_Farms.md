## Full job description

PURPOSE OF JOB:

To supervise the accounts payable department, ensure vendors are paid on time and act as a liaison between vendors and Highland Farms.

This position is on-site Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

Supervision of Accounts Payable Operations

Oversee daily processing of high-volume vendor invoices, credit notes, and payment requests
Ensure accurate three-way matching (Purchase Order, Receiving, Invoice)
Review and approve invoice coding to correct GL accounts, cost centers, and stores
Monitor AP aging reports and ensure timely payments to avoid late fees and service disruptions
Manage recurring payments, utilities, rent, and service contracts common in retail operations

Vendor Management

Maintain and manage vendor master data (setup, changes, compliance documentation)
Resolve invoice discrepancies, price variances, shortages, and vendor inquiries
Build and maintain strong relationships with suppliers and service providers
Ensure vendor statements are reconciled regularly

Team Supervision & Training

Supervise, train, and support Accounts Payable clerks and staff
Assign workloads, monitor productivity, and ensure adherence to deadlines
Review team work for accuracy, completeness, and compliance

Financial Controls & Compliance

Ensure compliance with internal controls, company policies, and audit requirements
Support internal and external audits by providing AP documentation and explanations
Ensure proper segregation of duties and approval workflows
Maintain compliance with tax requirements (HST/GST, withholding, etc.)

Month-End & Reporting

Prepare AP accruals and assist with month-end and year-end closing
Reconcile AP sub-ledger to the general ledger
Prepare AP reports including aging, vendor balances, and cash requirements

Other Requirements:

· Post-secondary diploma in accounting preferred.

·Knowledge of Sage 300 Accounting software

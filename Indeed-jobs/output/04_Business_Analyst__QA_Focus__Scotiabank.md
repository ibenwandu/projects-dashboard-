Purpose


We are seeking a Business Analyst with QA Responsibilities to join our Payments Technology team. This hybrid role combines core business analysis functions with hands-on functional and manual testing responsibilities. The successful candidate will work closely with stakeholders, technology teams, and QA specialists to ensure high-quality delivery of payment solutions in an Agile environment.


What You’ll Do:


Business Analysis Responsibilities

Lead the analysis, documentation, and validation of business requirements, ensuring all decisions and changes are properly captured.
Collaborate with business stakeholders and technology teams to deliver end-to-end solutions.
Translate complex business needs into clear and concise functional requirements and user stories.
Facilitate workshops and working sessions with cross-functional teams to gather and analyze requirements.
Perform gap analysis, root cause assessments, and release readiness reviews for payment system enhancements.
Act as a subject matter expert (SME) for payments-related projects and operational improvements.
Support Scrum Master and Project Manager in planning, scoping, and impact analysis for initiatives.


Quality Assurance Responsibilities (Functional/Manual)

Create, review, and execute functional test cases based on business requirements and user stories.
Perform manual testing for new features, enhancements, and regression cycles.
Collaborate with the development team to validate acceptance criteria and ensure quality standards are met.
Identify, document, and track defects; work with technical teams to resolve issues promptly.
Assist in planning and prioritizing testing activities across sprints.
Support release readiness by validating functionality in pre-production environments.


What You'll Bring:




Strong expertise in business process mapping, requirements gathering, and system analysis.
Experience in functional/manual testing within Agile projects.
Proficiency in Jira, Confluence, and MS Office tools.
Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with attention to detail.
Ability to communicate effectively with both technical and business stakeholders.
Knowledge of payment systems and standards (SWIFT, ISO 20022) is an asset.
Experience working in Scrum/Kanban environments.
Banking/Financial industry experience is a plus.


Working Conditions


Work in a standard office-based environment.

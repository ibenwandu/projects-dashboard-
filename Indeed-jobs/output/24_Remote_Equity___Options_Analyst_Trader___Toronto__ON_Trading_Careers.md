## Full job description

About the Firm

We are a well-established proprietary trading firm with over two decades of experience empowering traders to succeed. Our global team is driven by performance, discipline, and a passion for financial markets. As an independent contractor, you will enjoy the freedom to work remotely while building and managing your own trading strategies.

Position Overview

We are hiring remote equity and options analyst traders who combine fundamental research with active trading. You will analyze equity opportunities, develop options strategies around your thesis, and execute trades using firm-provided capital with full remote flexibility.

Location: Toronto, ON (Remote)

Employment Type: Independent Contractor (1099)

Experience Level: 2+ years trading or managing investments

Key Responsibilities
Develop and execute options strategies including directional, spread, and volatility plays
Analyze implied volatility, Greeks, and options pricing models to identify opportunities
Manage multi-leg options positions with disciplined risk protocols
Monitor and adjust open positions based on market conditions and Greeks changes
Maintain detailed logs of all options trades and strategy rationale
Optionally collaborate with a network of remote traders for insights and growth
Required Qualifications
At least 2 years of trading experience in options or currency markets
Keen understanding of options Greeks, volatility, or forex market dynamics
Discipline to adhere to a structured trading plan and risk management rules
Dedicated and coachable with a long-term commitment to developing your skills
Access to a reliable computer and high-speed internet for daily trading
Programming or quantitative skills are welcomed but not required
Position Details
Work as an independent contractor (1099) with full schedule flexibility
Keep 65–90% of your trading profits with increasing splits at higher levels
Trade with firm capital starting at up to $50,000, with scaling potential to $500,000+
100% remote — trade from anywhere with a reliable internet connection
Eligible for capital increases every two months as you demonstrate consistency
Compensation is entirely performance-based, giving you direct control over your earnings
Our Work Culture

We value autonomy, accountability, and results. You will operate in a remote-first environment where traders are empowered to craft their own strategies and optimize performance. Compensation is performance-driven, giving you direct control over your growth and earnings.

Location & Remote Work

This position is listed for candidates in the Toronto, ON area. While this is a fully remote role and you will work from your own location, we welcome applicants who are based in or near Toronto. All you need is a reliable computer and internet connection to get started.

Intro paragraph (optional):

Our client is seeking a Senior Data Analyst to join their Technology, Data & Digital Transformation team. This role focuses on advancing data enablement and visualization capabilities across the organization through strong analytics, robust data modeling, and impactful reporting solutions. The successful candidate will play a key role in strengthening data quality, governance, and performance insights across the business.

Principal tasks and responsibilities include:

Support and maintain data quality initiatives by tracing and validating ETL data pipelines, primarily using SQL
Translate non-technical business requirements into analytical designs that developers can build and support
Define and implement data validation and reconciliation frameworks for project data
Identify data quality gaps and embed validation checks into workflows
Develop and maintain performance calculation models including IRR, benchmarks, quartiles, and related financial metrics
Establish practical standards documentation and reporting templates to improve delivery efficiency
Design and deliver intuitive Power BI dashboards and reports that simplify complex metrics and support planning, prioritization, and performance tracking
Align business metrics across Data Engineering and Business Intelligence platforms
Support data governance initiatives by applying validation and cleansing procedures to maintain accurate and compliant datasets
Communicate insights and recommendations clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders

Qualifications and pre-requisites:

Bachelor's degree in Data Analytics, Statistics, Finance, Computer Science, or a related field
5+ years of experience in data analysis or Business Intelligence roles within financial services
Advanced Power BI skills including DAX, Power Query, data modeling, and dashboard design
Strong foundation in data validation, reconciliation, and quality assurance
Proficiency in SQL for data exploration and validation
Exceptional written and verbal communication skills
Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment
Strong time management and organizational skills
Preferred: Familiarity with Azure Data Lake, Synapse, or Fabric environments; experience leading internal upskilling initiatives; understanding of financial or operational performance metrics
Nice to have: Private capital industry knowledge, experience with data quality tools, and Python for data wrangling

## Full job description

Title: Data Analyst
Department: Customer Operations
Location: 6300 Steeles Ave West, Woodbridge
Total Potential Compensation: $65,000 - $90,000
Position Summary:
Provide support to Customer operations by creating dashboard and business models that present financial and customer data in order to facilitate effective decision-making and operational reporting. This position requires the preparation of dashboard, reports and analysis, development of SQL queries and automation of operational reports and processes.
Duties and Responsibilities:
Assisting the Team Manager of Analytics in updating and enhancing monthly and quarterly management reports and board slides
Develop new and refactor existing queries to extract information needed to support departmental and adhoc reports
Prepare detailed trend analysis, business models, and develop links that are critical in understanding accounts receivable, revenue, operating expenses, cash receipts and collections
Assess and make recommendations for Customer Operations to improve profitability, cash receipts and customer service
Develop and deliver automated operations reports and maintain or update as required
Perform and report on sensitivity and financial analysis to monitor and evaluate the financial performance of proposed cash receipt improvement and commercial account management strategies
Assist with the preparation of future oriented financial information (i.e. annual budget/forecasts) including Financial Operations costs, Revenue Reserves and Cash Receipts
Understand and analyze potential opportunities and risks and provide recommendations to management
Work with different areas of the business analyzing drivers and identifying upcoming financial issues or opportunities.
Create and update dashboards using Tableau
Identify areas of opportunity to make operations efficient through automation where possible
Improve business processes by providing new or enhanced financial reporting
Perform other duties as assigned

Qualifications:
Bachelor's Degree and/or Finance/Accounting Designation (or equivalent work experience) preferred (minimum 2 years of experience)
Relevant experience in a BI & reporting role
Additional courses in basic programming, SQL is considered an asset
An understanding of Accounting and Billing Systems and their related database structure
Proficiency in quantitative analysis
Work with different areas of the business analyzing drivers and identifying upcoming financial issues or opportunities
Commitment to personal growth and development
Good organization and leadership skills
In depth understanding of database analytics and related software and applications including BI Tools, MS Access, SQL Programming, and SAS data mining. Other Applications People Soft, MS Excel, PowerPoint, Word, Report Writing Software

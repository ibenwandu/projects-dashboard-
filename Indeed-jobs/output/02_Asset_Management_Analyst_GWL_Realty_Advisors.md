## Full job description

GWLRA is a leader in both the commercial and multi-residential real estate industries. We believe in generating value by creating vibrant, sustainable communities that engage, excite, and inspire. Our people live our values through the work they do and the results they deliver. Our core values are keeping our word, working better together and embracing diversity.

We're looking for a motivated Asset Management Analyst who will contribute to the day-to-day management and analysis of a growing portfolio and may include responsibility for a group of assets. This successful candidate will be a motivated, self-starter with a passion for real estate. Skills required in this position include a strong work ethic, well developed analytical and interpersonal skills and a team-oriented approach. Your areas of focus will include:
RESPONSIBILITIES
Planning & Strategy

Support the team in the preparation of annual budgets, interim forecasts, and business plans in collaboration with a variety of internal groups
Play an integral part in the annual appraisal process by liaising with external appraisers, providing pertinent information when required, and reviewing draft appraisals and corresponding valuation models
Present a variety of reports to senior asset management staff and portfolio management team
Valuation & Analysis

Build and maintain cash flow and valuation models by using a variety of approaches including discounted cash flow (DCF) analysis, net-present value (NPV) analysis, and cash-flow modelling
Using analytical tools when required to complete asset valuations, lease analyses, capital rationales, etc
Prepare analysis and reports for various internal and external parties
Work with internal teams during acquisition, disposition, and development phases and participate in the due diligence process
Lead appraisals and take an active role in the valuation process.
Evaluate return performance of assets and compare with market benchmark
Research, Presentation & Reporting

Keep current with market trends; prepare market reports and research papers for internal use
Prepare presentations to external clients; including mid-year review presentations and budget presentations
Provide support to the asset management team on special projects as needed
QUALIFICATIONS

Minimum of 1-3 years experience gained from a similarly positioned role in commercial and/or residential asset management, investment analysis, brokerage or appraisal
Post-secondary education in a related discipline, CFA Charterholder/candidacy or AACI Designation/candidacy is an asset.
Proficiency in Microsoft Excel; Office; PowerPoint and Outlook. Experience with Argus Enterprise is an asset
Motivated self-starter that can work independently and navigate through ambiguous tasks
Excellent quantitative, analytical, financial modelling, problem-solving and researching skills with strong attention to detail
Ability to communicate confidently and clearly with a variety of internal and external stakeholders
